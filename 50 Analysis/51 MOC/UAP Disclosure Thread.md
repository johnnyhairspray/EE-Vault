# UAP Disclosure Thread

## Status as of April 2026

The UAP disclosure ecosystem is at its most institutionally active point since the 2017 New York Times revelation of the Pentagon's AATIP program. Congressional pressure, a White House directive, and a Pentagon stonewalling pattern are converging simultaneously with the Mondaloy Chain cluster and confirmed operational hypersonic testing. ## The 46 Videos Standoff — April 2026

**March 31, 2026:** Rep. Anna Paulina Luna — chairwoman of the House Oversight Task Force on the Declassification of Federal Secrets — sent Secretary of War Pete Hegseth the most specific UAP disclosure demand in congressional history. Rather than requesting categories of information, the letter named 46 individual video files by title, date, location, and in some cases military callsign. This eliminated the standard government wiggle room of claiming no responsive records exist.

Sources told the New York Post the footage includes material that will "fundamentally challenge public understanding of what the military has been observing in restricted airspace." The footage allegedly shows objects performing maneuvers defying known physics, operating in restricted military airspace, and in some cases being engaged by American fighters.

**April 14, 2026:** The Pentagon missed the deadline entirely. Luna stated no one responded until her office followed up, and that "it appears someone did not pass the letter to the appropriate authorities." She described this as the same institutional resistance pattern the task force has encountered throughout its investigation.

**April 17, 2026:** Trump stated at a Turning Point USA event in Phoenix that "very interesting documents" will be released "very, very soon" — his first public acknowledgment that the review has found something worth noting.

**April 17, 2026 (same day):** Luna threatened subpoena authority if Pentagon cooperation is not forthcoming. She described Hegseth as a "close working partner aligned with Trump's directive" — suggesting the resistance is coming from the institutional layers below political leadership, not from the top.

**AARO's pattern:** Luna explicitly faulted AARO, saying its responses "when questioned about UAP sightings and provided data" have been "less than adequate." A September 9, 2025 House hearing heard whistleblower testimony that AARO possesses additional video records it has not disclosed.

**This section is speculative analysis, not established fact. Confidence: Low-Medium. Worth documenting precisely because it is not being documented elsewhere.**

The UAP disclosure ecosystem and the Mondaloy Chain cluster are occurring simultaneously. The analytical question: are UAP reports from military pilots observing classified hypersonic test vehicles?

**The physics match:**

**The institutional behavior match:**

This is precisely the behavior you'd expect if the videos show classified hypersonic test vehicles rather than alien craft. **The narrative utility:**

Allowing the alien hypothesis to dominate UAP discourse serves the hypersonic program in multiple ways:
