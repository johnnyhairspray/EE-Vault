# The Sovcorp Overlay: Neocameralism and the Board of Peace

**Status: Working hypothesis. Structural parallels are documented. Direct causal lineage is unestablished. Yarvin himself acknowledges the convergence.**

## The Theory

[[Curtis Yarvin]]'s neocameralism proposes replacing democratic governance with a **sovereign corporation (sovcorp)** — a joint-stock entity that owns and operates a territory. Key structural features:

- **CEO-monarch** with unchallenged executive authority, unelected and irremovable
- **Shareholders** (large capital holders) hold power, not citizens
- **Residents** are customers or guests — contractual, not sovereign
- **No democratic accountability** — efficiency replaces consent
- **Private military** enforces property rights and deters aggression
- **Exit over voice** — dissatisfied residents leave, they do not vote
- Territory managed as **profit center**, not public trust

Yarvin cites Dubai and Singapore as prototype sovcorps. He explicitly proposed turning Gaza into **"Gaza Inc."** — a publicly listed charter city with stock symbol GAZA, funded by IPO, residents compensated with shares contingent on displacement.

## The Structure of the Board of Peace

The [[Board of Peace]], established January 17, 2026, maps onto the sovcorp model with striking precision:

| Neocameralist Feature | BoP Implementation |
|---|---|
| CEO-monarch, no term limits | [[Donald Trump]], Chairman for Life, sole succession authority |
| Shareholder governance | Executive Board: [[Jared Kushner]], [[Steve Witkoff]], [[Ajay Banga]], Gulf sovereign wealth |
| No democratic accountability | Independent of UN, PA, and standard international frameworks |
| Private military arm | [[International Stabilization Force]] — accountable to the Board, not any democratic government |
| Residents as contractual guests | "Terror-free zones" with biometric surveillance, checkpoint control, monitored purchases |
| Capital as the equity stake | $70B reconstruction capital, [[USD1]] stablecoin as designated payment instrument |
| Permanent membership via capital | $1 billion buys permanent Board membership |

## The Yarvin Connection

Yarvin explicitly proposed "Gaza Inc." — predating the BoP's formation. When asked about the structural similarities, he stated he never shared his proposal with Trump directly, attributing the convergence to them "inhabiting the same reality."

That framing is worth sitting with. It does not claim coordination. It claims **ideological alignment so deep that independent actors arrive at structurally identical solutions**. This is consistent with how Yarvin's ideas propagate — not as a memo passed down a chain of command, but as a conceptual operating system installed in the minds of [[Peter Thiel]], [[10 Entities/11 People/11.1 Political/JD Vance]], and the broader network before any specific policy decision is made.

The lineage: Yarvin theorizes the sovcorp → Thiel funds the network that installs Vance → Vance is VP when the BoP is designed → Kushner (Thiel-adjacent, Gulf-connected) sits on the Executive Board → the structure that emerges is functionally indistinguishable from a sovcorp.

## What the BoP Is Not

It stops short of Yarvin's full vision in two ways:

1. It is not formally a **publicly listed corporation** — Gaza is not trading on NASDAQ
2. Displacement has not been formally codified as the mechanism for share distribution

Both gaps may be temporary. The stablecoin infrastructure, biometric control zones, and $1B membership buy-in suggest the financial architecture is being built before the legal form is named.

## The Colonial Trading Company Parallel

The Naked Capitalism analysis frames the BoP not as a neocameralist sovcorp per se, but as something **functionally reminiscent of Western colonial trading companies** — the Dutch East India Company, the East India Company — private capital structures with diplomatic immunity, sovereign territory, and private military enforcement. This framing may be more precise than the sovcorp label, while arriving at the same analytical conclusion: **post-democratic capital infrastructure operating above state accountability**.

## What Remains Unestablished

- Direct evidence Yarvin's Gaza Inc. proposal was read by BoP architects
- Whether the $1B membership structure was deliberately modeled on shareholder logic or arrived at independently
- Whether [[USD1]] stablecoin integration is operational or still planned
- Long-term displacement intent — the BoP formally denies forced removal
