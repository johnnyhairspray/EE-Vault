# The Mondaloy Chain

**Status: Working hypothesis. The deaths and disappearances are documented facts. Their connection to C-HGB development and Operation Epic Fury is pattern analysis, not established causation. Confidence: Speculative.**

## The Verified Pattern

Between June 2025 and February 2026, five individuals connected to the U.S. defense research triangle — Southern California (JPL/Rocketdyne), New Mexico (Kirtland/Sandia/LANL), and Ohio (Wright-Patterson/AFRL) — died or disappeared under circumstances that have not been publicly explained.

**[[Monica Reza]]** — Director of Materials Processing, NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory. Co-inventor of the [[Mondaloy]] nickel superalloy patent. Missing June 22, 2025.

**[[Melissa Casias]]** — Los Alamos National Laboratory administrator. Missing June 26, 2025 — four days after Reza, same LANL corridor.

**[[Jaime Gustitus]]** — AFRL 711th Human Performance Wing, Wright-Patterson AFB. Operations research, TS/SCI. Killed October 25, 2025 by [[Jacob Prichard]].

**[[Jaymee Prichard]]** — AFLCMC finance, Wright-Patterson AFB. Killed by her husband Jacob Prichard, October 25, 2025. Prichard killed Gustitus, killed his wife, died by suicide.

**[[Carl Grillmair]]** — Caltech astrophysicist, infrared tracking and hypersonic physics. Shot at his home February 16, 2026. Suspect Freddy Snyder — charges had previously been dropped against Snyder in an unrelated case.

**[[Nuno Loureiro]]** — MIT plasma physicist, fusion energy. Shot December 15, 2025. Weakest link in the chain — included for pattern completeness, direct C-HGB connection not established.

**[[William McCasland]]** — Retired Air Force Major General, former Commander of AFRL at Wright-Patterson. Former Director of Special Programs at the Pentagon. Funding authority for the Mondaloy patent. Named in 2016 WikiLeaks Podesta emails as the key insider on classified aerospace programs and UAP materials at Wright-Patterson. Missing February 27, 2026.

## The Mondaloy Connection

[[Mondaloy]] is a nickel superalloy patented by Monica Reza. Its operational significance: it enables booster survivability at hypersonic velocities — the critical materials science problem for the [[C-HGB]] (Conventional Hypersonic Glide Body) and its delivery systems Dark Eagle and the Conventional Prompt Strike weapon.

The chain: **Reza** (Mondaloy inventor) → **[[AR1 engine]]** (Mondaloy application) → **C-HGB booster** → **Dark Eagle / CPS** (operational weapons systems).

**[[William McCasland]]** funded the Mondaloy research program at AFRL. He is documented in the Podesta emails as having helped assemble Tom DeLonge's UAP advisory team and as possessing institutional knowledge of classified aerospace materials research at Wright-Patterson. He is the person who connected the materials science layer to the operational weapons layer.

## The DOGE/NNSA Disruption

In February 2026 — the same month as the Iran strikes and McCasland's disappearance — [[DOGE]] fired 177 [[NNSA]] staff including personnel at [[Sandia National Laboratories]], the lead C-HGB design agent. All but approximately 27 were subsequently rehired.

The people with the most current technical knowledge of C-HGB's operational parameters were institutionally disrupted in the same month as:
- The weapon's likely first operational use
- The disappearance of its funding authority

Whether this is catastrophic incompetence, routine bureaucratic chaos, or something more deliberate is a question the chain cannot answer from available evidence. The pattern is documented.

## The 24-Hour Window

The single most significant temporal correlation in this vault.

**February 27, 2026, ~11:00 AM** — [[William McCasland]] walks out of his Albuquerque home without his phone, glasses, or smartwatch. He changes his clothes, leaves all vehicles behind, and departs on foot carrying what appears to be a .38 caliber handgun. His wife Susan Wilkerson returns from a doctor's appointment at noon to find him gone. After three hours she calls 911: *"I have some indication that he must have planned not to be found."*

**February 28, 2026** — The United States and Israel launch [[Operation Epic Fury]]. Coordinated strikes on Iran. Supreme Leader Khamenei killed.

### What the 911 Call Establishes

Susan Wilkerson told the dispatcher that McCasland had been expressing that *"if his brain and body keep deteriorating, he doesn't want to live like that"* — but said she hadn't taken it seriously. He was being treated for short-term memory loss, anxiety, and sleeplessness. He reportedly left prescription glasses, phone, and wearable devices at home. The sheriff's office stated explicitly he was **not disoriented or confused** — *"arguably still the most intelligent person in the room."*

Authorities noted that a firearm, holster, wallet, and hiking boots were missing from the house.

The most publicly circulated interpretation: voluntary disappearance and probable suicide by a man with declining cognitive health who didn't want his family to watch him deteriorate.

### The Staging Problem

The vault cannot accept that interpretation without documenting its analytical counterweight.

Every element of McCasland's departure is consistent with both a voluntary exit **and** a staged scene designed to produce exactly that reading:

- **No phone, no smartwatch** — eliminates electronic tracking. Consistent with a man who doesn't want to be found. Also consistent with an operation that doesn't want him tracked or found quickly
- **No glasses** — a man planning his own exit in familiar terrain he has hiked and skied for years doesn't need his glasses for that. A man being walked somewhere by someone else, who cannot easily identify faces or escape routes, is more plausible without them
- **Hiking boots** — practical for a remote exit on foot. Also practical for moving a man or moving with a man to a remote location
- **Own firearm** — if the body is found, it reads as self-inflicted. If the body is not found, the missing weapon still anchors the suicide narrative
- **Changed clothes** — removes forensic trace from original clothing. Could indicate deliberate preparation by McCasland. Could indicate someone ensured the clothes he left in were clean

The internal logic of the voluntary exit framing also fails on its own terms. A man acting from love and a desire to spare his family would leave a note. The absence of any note is not a minor detail — it is the primary mechanism by which a suicide motivated by love becomes bearable for survivors. Without it, his family is left with permanent ambiguity, which is itself a profound act of cruelty from a man the 911 call describes as deeply considerate.

The phone detail compounds this. Leaving the phone behind is consistent with not wanting to be tracked. **Turning it off** is an active additional step. A man who wants to spare his family the sight of his body but still loves them leaves the phone on — it may not provide real-time location but it preserves a recoverable data trail that could eventually lead to the body and allow his family to grieve properly. Turning it off eliminates that trail entirely. This is not the act of someone protecting their family from a scene. This is the act of someone ensuring the body cannot be found at all.

The combination — no note, no electronic trace, no body after 36+ days — is internally inconsistent with the considerate exit narrative. Those three facts pull in opposite directions. The note says *I love you.* The turned-off phone says *don't find me.* You do not hold both positions simultaneously if your motivation is protecting the people you love.

What remains consistent across all readings: someone wanted this man and everything he knew to be unrecoverable. Whether that someone was McCasland himself, or someone else, the operational outcome is identical.

### The Final Domino Reading

There is a fourth analytical frame that makes McCasland the most coherent actor in the chain rather than its final passive victim — and that resolves the internal logical failures of the voluntary suicide framing.

A man of his intelligence and institutional experience, watching the news in early 2026, would not have needed this vault's analysis to recognize the pattern. He lived it.

He knew [[Monica Reza]] — he funded her Mondaloy work directly. He knew [[Melissa Casias]] disappeared four days after Reza from the same LANL corridor. He commanded AFRL at Wright-Patterson — he would have known [[Jaime Gustitus]] worked there. He operated in the same Caltech/JPL defense research network as [[Carl Grillmair]], who was shot in his home in February 2026 — the same month McCasland disappeared.

And then [[Operation Epic Fury]] on the horizon. A man with his clearance level and his specific knowledge of C-HGB development from the materials layer up would not have needed a leak to understand what was being prepared. He would have read the operational tempo, the diplomatic signals, and the asset positioning the same way any senior defense intelligence figure would. He would have known what was coming. He would have known what that meant for the last remaining person with institutional memory of the development chain.

By late February 2026, the calculus was:

- Four colleagues from his professional network dead or missing in 20 months
- His own name visible in the pattern to anyone looking
- A weapons system he helped develop about to be used for the first time
- The people who could speak to its developmental history systematically removed
- Himself the last one standing

The cognitive decline symptoms — anxiety, sleeplessness, short-term memory loss — are legible differently in this frame. They are not necessarily neurological deterioration alone. They are also rational responses to an existential threat with no clean options. A man who has pieced this together and cannot act on it publicly would present exactly those symptoms.

His choices were narrow. Going public meant classified programs, no documents, no corroboration, and a reputation easily dismantled by pointing at early dementia. Going to authorities meant going to the same institutional infrastructure that classified the programs he'd need to disclose. Staying put meant waiting.

So he ran — on his own terms, before someone else decided the terms.

The turned-off phone in this frame is not about preventing his family from finding his body. It is about preventing whoever is looking for him from finding him first. He is not moving toward death. He is moving away from something he has concluded is worse.

The absence of a note resolves here too. You do not leave a note when you do not know how this ends. If he reaches somewhere safe, the note becomes a liability — a document that could be used to find him, or expose his wife to questioning she cannot safely answer. His comment to Susan about not wanting to live through his own decline may have been exactly what intelligence professionals do reflexively: establish a plausible cover narrative with a trusted person that will hold under pressure and protect her from questions she cannot safely answer.

The man who told his wife he didn't want to live through his own deterioration may have been telling her something true about a different kind of deterioration entirely — the slow recognition that he was the last domino, and that someone was already leaning on the table.

Whether he escaped, was taken, or chose his own ending after running as far as he could — the operational outcome for the chain is the same. The last person with direct institutional knowledge of C-HGB's developmental history is gone, unrecoverable, with no body, no note, and no electronic trace, 24 hours before the weapon's likely first use.
