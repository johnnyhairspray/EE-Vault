# The Mondaloy Chain

**Status: Working hypothesis. All node facts verified. Causal connections are inference.**

## The Verified Pattern

Nine months. Nine scientists and defense personnel. Dead or missing. Seven connected to the Air Force. Three geographic nodes — Southern California, New Mexico, Ohio — the exact triangle of American defense aerospace research.

The chain begins with material and ends with silence.

## The Chain

**[[Mondaloy]]** solves the metallurgical problem blocking oxygen-rich staged combustion engines. Without it, the [[C-HGB]] booster cannot survive its operating environment. Without C-HGB, America has no operational hypersonic weapon.

**[[Monica Reza]]** holds the patent. Vanished June 22, 2025.

**[[Melissa Casias]]** works at [[Los Alamos National Laboratory]], four miles from where [[William McCasland]] will later disappear. Vanishes four days after Reza, June 26, 2025.

**[[Jaime Gustitus]]** models aerospace trajectories with TS/SCI clearance inside AFRL. Killed October 25, 2025 by [[Jacob Prichard]], who managed advanced sensor acquisition programs at the same institution.

**[[Nuno Loureiro]]** leads plasma physics research approaching fusion breakthrough. Shot December 15, 2025. Weakest direct link to hypersonic chain — included for chronological completeness.

**[[Carl Grillmair]]** tracks atmospheric re-entry objects using the same infrared physics as hypersonic missile detection systems. Works in the same JPL institutional corridor as Reza. Shot February 16, 2026. His charges against his killer were dropped weeks before the killing.

**[[William McCasland]]** controlled the funding that built [[Mondaloy]] into operational weapons systems. Vanishes February 27, 2026 — leaving phone, glasses, wearables behind.

**Operation Epic Fury begins February 28, 2026.**

## The Operational Hypothesis

**Confidence: Speculative. No direct evidence of US weapons use in Iran conflict.**

Terminal phase signature of [[C-HGB]] — unpowered descent, Mach 10+ plasma sheath, near-vertical trajectory — produces a "fireball straight down" visual with no exhaust trail, no sound signature, and no radar-trackable boost phase. Consistent with eyewitness and video reports from strikes during Operation Epic Fury attributed to no visible propulsion.

[[C-HGB]] is sub-orbital boost-glide, not LEO kinetic. It transits through upper atmosphere without orbiting — exploiting the Outer Space Treaty loophole prohibiting orbital weapons while remaining legally ambiguous as a conventional prompt strike system.

Iron Dome and David's Sling are designed for ballistic trajectories. Neither is optimized for Mach 10+ near-vertical kinetic penetrators. Consistent with reported intercept failures during the conflict.

Sub-meter precision on hardened buried targets — including the opening strike — is consistent with C-HGB terminal guidance capabilities demonstrated in December 2024 testing.

The people who could confirm US material signatures in post-strike debris are gone before the first strike.

## What The Pattern Suggests

The people who could identify [[Mondaloy]]'s material signature in post-strike debris analysis — or verify its presence in weapons used during the conflict — are gone before the first strike.

This is not about eliminating people who built the weapon. It is about eliminating people who could identify it after use.
