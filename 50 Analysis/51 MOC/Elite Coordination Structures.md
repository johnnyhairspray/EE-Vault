# Elite Coordination Structures: Secret Societies and the Network

**⚠️ Methodology note:** This MOC applies strict confidence tiering. The documented connections between these structures and the broader network are significant and don't require conspiracy framing to be alarming. Undocumented claims (Illuminati, Satanic rituals, world government plots) are excluded. What remains after discipline is still substantial.

## The Structures

### Skull and Bones (Order 322)
**Founded:** 1832, Yale University  
**Confidence: Verified**

Senior secret society at Yale. 15 members selected per year from the junior class. Officially operates as the **Russell Trust Association** (named after co-founder William Huntington Russell). $17M endowment as of 2024.

**The CIA pipeline — documented:**

Bonesmen F. Trubee Davison served as the CIA's director of personnel, and James Jesus Angleton was the longtime head of CIA counterintelligence. This is documented, not speculated. A Yale secret society produced the CIA's personnel director and its counterintelligence chief. At Yale, secret society members were historically called "Spooks" — decades before the term became CIA slang.

**Documented alumni with intelligence/power connections:**
- **George H.W. Bush (1948)** — 41st President, 11th Director of Central Intelligence (CIA Director). His Bones nickname: "Magog."
- **George W. Bush (1968)** — 43rd President
- **John Kerry (1966)** — Secretary of State, 2004 presidential candidate
- **William F. Buckley Jr. (1950)** — founder of National Review, former CIA officer
- **James Jesus Angleton (1941)** — CIA counterintelligence chief for nearly two decades
- **F. Trubee Davison (1918)** — CIA Director of Personnel
- **William Sloane Coffin (1949)** — CIA agent 1950-1953
- **Robert A. Lovett (1918)** — US Secretary of Defense
- **Henry Luce (1920)** — co-founder of Time-Life
- **Charles Seymour (1908)** — President of Yale, **founding member of the Council on Foreign Relations**

**The founding money — documented but underreported:**
The Russell Trust Association takes its name from co-founder William Huntington Russell, whose family firm Russell & Company was the largest American opium smuggler operating in China in the 19th century. The society's initial endowment was built on opium trade profits. This is not conspiracy theory — it is documented commercial history.

**The 2004 election:** Both presidential candidates — George W. Bush (R) and John Kerry (D) — were Skull and Bones members. When asked about it in separate interviews, both declined to discuss it.

### Bohemian Grove
**Founded:** 1872 (Bohemian Club)  
**Confidence: Verified for membership/existence; ritual details partially verified**

Annual two-week summer encampment in a redwood forest in Monte Rio, California, hosted by the **Bohemian Club** (San Francisco, founded 1872 by journalists, evolved into ultra-exclusive men's club). Approximately 2,000-3,000 members including some of the world's most powerful political and corporate figures.

**Documented membership/attendance:**
- Herbert Hoover, Richard Nixon, Ronald Reagan, both President Bushes — confirmed
- Koch brothers — confirmed
- Donald Rumsfeld — confirmed
- Harlan Crow (brought Supreme Court Justice Clarence Thomas) — confirmed
- Kevin McCarthy — reportedly gave speech at 2023 Grove predicting Trump as 2024 GOP candidate

**The Cremation of Care ritual — partially verified:**
Alex Jones filmed the ceremony in 1992, which shows: hooded robes, torches, a burning effigy in a coffin, a 40-foot stone owl (Moloch). The club describes this as a theatrical performance symbolizing members leaving their worldly cares behind for the retreat. The footage is real. The interpretation varies wildly. The theatrical/symbolic reading is the most defensible.

**Why it matters without conspiracy framing:** The most powerful men in American political and corporate life spend two weeks together annually in a private forest, completely off the record, for 150+ years. No press. No record. The **Manhattan Project was discussed at Bohemian Grove** before being officially proposed — documented. Policy is informally shaped before it becomes official.

**Connection to the network:** Functions as the American equivalent of Bilderberg's social/bonding layer — less formal, more recreational, but accomplishing the same informal coordination function. Bohemian Grove is right-leaning; Bilderberg is more transatlantic/centrist.

### Trilateral Commission
**Founded:** 1973, by David Rockefeller  
**Confidence: Verified**

Founded to foster cooperation between North America, Europe, and Japan/Asia-Pacific. Three regional chairs: Washington D.C., Paris, Tokyo. Members include politicians, business executives, academics, and media figures.

**Why it matters:** The Trilateral Commission is more transparent than Bilderberg — it publishes membership lists and some reports. But it serves an identical coordinating function: pre-aligning elite consensus across three regions before it becomes official policy.

**Documented alumni:**
- Jimmy Carter (member before presidency)
- Walter Mondale
- Zbigniew Brzezinski (founder alongside Rockefeller)
- Dick Cheney
- Multiple Fed chairs, Treasury secretaries, and national security advisors

**Connection to vault:** David Petraeus — Bilderberg steering committee member — represents the same intelligence-to-private-capital pipeline that connects these structures. The Trilateral Commission, Bilderberg, and Council on Foreign Relations form a three-tier coordination stack: CFR (intellectual/policy layer), Trilateral (regional coordination), Bilderberg (transatlantic private consensus).

### Council on Foreign Relations (CFR)
**Founded:** 1921  
**Confidence: Verified**

The most transparent and intellectually legitimate of these structures. Publishes *Foreign Affairs*, produces policy papers, has ~5,000 members. But its function in the network is significant:

- Founded partly by Skull and Bones member Charles Seymour
- Every Secretary of State since the 1940s has been a CFR member or fellow
- Every CIA director since the 1950s has had CFR connections
- Produces the intellectual framework that Bilderberg then coordinates around privately

CFR is the think tank layer. Bilderberg is the private coordination layer. The distinction matters for confidence tiering — CFR's influence is exercised publicly through publications and testimony, not in secret.
