# Executive Order: Designating Antifa as a Domestic Terrorist Organization

Signed September 22, 2025. Labels Antifa a "militarist, anarchist enterprise" and designates it as a domestic terrorist organization. See [[Antifa]] vault note for the full legal analysis and structural problem with this designation.

## Key Provisions

**Section 1:** Characterizes Antifa as a militarist anarchist enterprise seeking to overthrow the government and suppress lawful political activity through violence, intimidation, and doxing.

**Section 2:** Orders all applicable federal departments to investigate, disrupt, and dismantle Antifa operations and funding sources using all available authorities.

**Section 3:** Initiates investigation into donors and financial conduits supporting Antifa — creating the legal basis for treating financial contributions to broadly associated organizations as potential material support for domestic terrorism.

## The Legal Problem

As documented in the [[Antifa]] note: the domestic terrorism statute (18 U.S.C. § 2331) has no formal designation mechanism equivalent to the State Department's foreign terrorist organization list. There is no legal process by which a loosely affiliated ideological movement without centralized structure can be formally designated. The EO creates a political label; the legal framework for acting on it is ambiguous.

The practical effect is enforcement-level: FBI and DOJ field offices apply the label to individuals regardless of the legal ambiguity, changing enforcement behavior without requiring court validation.

Combined with [[NSPM-7]]'s expansion of domestic terrorism definitions to include anti-capitalism as an ideology, the EO operationalizes viewpoint-based policing — treating ideological alignment rather than specific violent acts as the triggering condition for terrorism-level enforcement.
