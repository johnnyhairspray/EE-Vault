# Executive Order: Designating Antifa as a Domestic Terrorist Organization

## 🔍 Summary
The formal executive order that designates **[[Antifa]]** as a "domestic terrorist organization". It provides the legal authority for federal departments to utilize all applicable powers to "investigate, disrupt, and dismantle" its operations.

## 🧬 The "Trigger" Piston
* **Section 1 (The Threat):** Labels Antifa a "militarist, anarchist enterprise" seeking to overthrow the government and suppress lawful political speech through violence and doxing.
* **Section 2 (The Mandate):** Orders the use of all "applicable authorities" to stop coordinated efforts designed to achieve policy objectives by "coercion and intimidation".
* **Section 3 (Funding):** Specifically targets the funding sources behind Antifa operations, initiating a "thorough investigation" into donors and financial conduits.
