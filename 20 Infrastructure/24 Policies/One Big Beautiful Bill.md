# One Big Beautiful Bill (OBBB)

## 🔍 Summary
H.R. 1, known as the **One Big Beautiful Bill Act**, is the definitive spending and tax legislation of the second Trump term. Passed through the budget reconciliation process to avoid the Senate filibuster, it allocates over **$126 billion** specifically for border and interior enforcement—roughly seven times the previous annual budget for ICE.

## 🧬 The "Legislative" Piston

### 1. The Enforcement Surge
* **Detention Expansion:** Allocates **$45 billion** for a massive expansion of detention capacity, including family units and the construction of **[[Accountability Centers]]**.
* **Agent Hiring:** Provides funding to hire **12,000 new ICE officers** and agents, representing a 120% increase in interior enforcement personnel by 2026.
* **Wall Construction:** Obligates **$46.5 billion** for the completion of the border wall and waterborne barriers in the Rio Grande Valley.

### 2. The "Self-Funding" Fee Model
The bill implements aggressive, non-waivable fees to make the immigration system "pay for itself":
* **Asylum Fee:** $100 initial filing fee + $100 annual "maintenance fee" for every year a case remains pending.
* **Apprehension Fee:** A **$5,000 surcharge** assessed against individuals apprehended between ports of entry or ordered removed in absentia.
* **Work Permits:** Initial employment authorization fees increased to **$550** (non-waivable).

### 3. Public Benefit Revocation
* **Medicaid/CHIP:** Revokes access to healthcare benefits for all "newcomers" effective **October 1, 2026**.
* **SNAP Expansion:** Expands work requirements for food assistance and shifts the cost-burden of these programs onto the states.

## 🔗 The "Technate" Loop
* **The Intelligence Link:** Authorizes the DHS Secretary to set "minimal detention standards" without judicial review, enabling the rapid conversion of commercial warehouses into **[[Facilities]]**.
* **The Targeting Link:** Provides $6.2 billion for "Screening Technology," including the AI and machine learning kernels that power **[[ImmigrationOS]]** and **[[ELITE]]**.
