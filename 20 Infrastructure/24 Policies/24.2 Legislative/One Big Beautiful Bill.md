# One Big Beautiful Bill (OBBB)

H.R. 1, the One Big Beautiful Bill Act. Passed through budget reconciliation to avoid the Senate filibuster. Signed July 4, 2025. The primary legislative instrument of the second Trump term's immigration enforcement agenda.

## Enforcement Funding ($126B+)

- **$45 billion** for detention capacity expansion including family detention units and new facility construction
- **$46.5 billion** for border wall completion and waterborne barriers in the Rio Grande Valley
- **12,000 new ICE officers** funded — approximately 120% increase in interior enforcement personnel
- **$6.2 billion** for AI and screening technology including ImmigrationOS and ELITE infrastructure

## The Fee Model

The bill implements non-waivable fees designed to make the immigration system "self-funding":

- **$100 asylum filing fee** plus $100 annual maintenance fee per year a case remains pending
- **$5,000 surcharge** against individuals apprehended between ports of entry or ordered removed in absentia
- **$550 work permit fee** (non-waivable)

The practical effect of fees imposed on people who often cannot pay them: accumulated debt creating additional grounds for enforcement action and removing financial access to legal remedies.

## Public Benefit Revocation

**Medicaid/CHIP:** Access to healthcare for "newcomers" — broadly defined — revoked effective October 1, 2026.

**SNAP:** Expanded work requirements and state cost-shifting for food assistance.

## The Detention Standards Provision

Authorizes the DHS Secretary to set "minimal detention standards" without judicial review — enabling rapid conversion of commercial warehouses into detention facilities. This provision accelerated the $38.3 billion "Detention Reengineering Initiative" documented in the [[ICE]] vault note.

## Why Elon Musk Broke With Trump

The OBBB's deficit expansion was the documented trigger for Musk's public split with Trump in May 2025 and his departure from DOGE. Musk publicly criticized the bill on X as fiscally irresponsible. Trump responded by attacking Musk. The break was brief but documented.
