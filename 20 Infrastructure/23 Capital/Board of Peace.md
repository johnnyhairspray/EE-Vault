# Board of Peace

Supreme oversight body for the postwar transition of the Gaza Strip, formally established January 17, 2026. Operates outside standard UN or Palestinian Authority constraints — a parallel governance structure with absolute authority over subsidiary entities in the territory.

## Structure

[[Donald Trump]] serves as Chairman for life with sole authority to nominate his successor — no term limits, no democratic accountability mechanism. [[Nickolay Mladenov]] serves as High Representative managing daily operations. Executive Board includes [[Marco Rubio]], [[Jared Kushner]], and World Bank President [[Ajay Banga]] — a business-first reconstruction model with direct financial extraction architecture.

## Why This Is Infrastructure, Not Politics

The BoP is not a political organization in the conventional sense. It is post-state capital infrastructure — private and sovereign-adjacent power operating above democratic accountability. Chairman for life. Independent of UN constraints. Controlling billions in reconstruction capital flowing through a territory with no functioning government.

The financial layer — UAE capitalization, reconstruction contracts, potential [[USD1]] stablecoin integration for disbursement — makes this less a governance body than a sovereign wealth extraction mechanism with a humanitarian facade.

## The ISF Connection

The [[International Stabilization Force]] is the BoP's military arm. A private military force accountable not to any democratic government but to the Board's chairman. This is the armed enforcement mechanism of post-democratic capital infrastructure.
