# NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory

Federally funded research and development center managed by the California Institute of Technology for NASA. Located in Pasadena, California. Primary mission covers robotic space exploration, Earth observation, and advanced aerospace research.

Institutional geography is significant: JPL and Caltech's Infrared Processing and Analysis Center (IPAC) share the same San Gabriel Valley corridor. [[Monica Reza]] moved to JPL after Aerojet Rocketdyne. [[Carl Grillmair]] worked at IPAC processing NEO Surveyor data developed by JPL. Same campus ecosystem — different buildings, overlapping institutional networks.

NEO Surveyor, developed at JPL, uses long-wavelength infrared detection to track objects during atmospheric transit — the same physics underlying military hypersonic missile tracking systems.

NASA made no public statement about Reza's disappearance. Institutional silence consistent across all nodes in this cluster.
