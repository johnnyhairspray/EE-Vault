# Kirtland Air Force Base

Air Force installation in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Home to [[Sandia National Laboratories]] — the lead design agent for the [[C-HGB]] hypersonic glide body. Also hosts the Air Force Nuclear Weapons Center and the Air Force Space Command's Space Operations Center.

Kirtland is the anchor of the New Mexico node of the defense aerospace research triangle (New Mexico / Southern California / Ohio), alongside [[Los Alamos National Laboratory]] 35 miles to the northwest.

[[William McCasland]] — who commanded the AFRL Space Vehicles Directorate and later went missing on February 27, 2026 — lived in Albuquerque, placing him in the immediate geographic vicinity of Kirtland and Sandia.
