# Wright-Patterson Air Force Base

One of the largest and most important U.S. Air Force installations. Home to the [[Air Force Research Laboratory]], the primary institution responsible for developing and transitioning advanced aerospace technologies into the defense arsenal — including hypersonic weapons, sensors, and directed energy systems.

Three geographic nodes anchor American defense aerospace research: Southern California (JPL/Rocketdyne), New Mexico (Kirtland/Sandia), and Ohio (Wright-Patterson/AFRL). On October 25, 2025, all three personnel killed in the Prichard murder-suicide worked at this installation.

Also associated with classified UAP programs. [[William McCasland]] held commands connected to Wright-Patterson's research pipeline.
