# Accountability Centers

## 🔍 Summary
The physical "End-State" for individuals removed from the public grid under **[[14321 - Ending Crime and Disorder on America's Streets]]**. These are high-security, federalized facilities where release is contingent on "clinical compliance" rather than a legal sentence.

## 🧬 The "Facility" Piston
* **The Civil Commitment Bypass:** Residents are held under "Civil Commitment," which avoids Sixth Amendment requirements for a speedy trial. This allows for **Indefinite Detention** as long as a subject is deemed a "risk to public disorder".
* **Operational Synchronization:** Managed via a hybrid model where medical staff provide "treatment" while **[[ICE]]** or federal contractors provide security and movement control.
* **NSPM-7 Integration:** Under **[[NSPM-7]]**, these centers are authorized to hold "non-violent" political dissidents flagged with "behavioral health markers" associated with radicalization, effectively bypassing the standard bail process.
