# Conventional Prompt Strike (CPS)

U.S. Navy's hypersonic weapons program. Delivers the [[C-HGB]] (Common Hypersonic Glide Body) from Zumwalt-class destroyers. The naval counterpart to the Army's [[Dark Eagle]] — both systems use the same C-HGB glide body, different launch platforms and boosters.

**Platform:** The USS Zumwalt (DDG-1000) class — the Navy's advanced destroyer. The Zumwalt completed a dry-dock conversion in early 2026 removing its failed 155mm Advanced Gun System and replacing the space with large-diameter tubes for C-HGB launch.

**Status (2026):** Navy CPS still completing integration and testing. Not yet declared fully operational as of March 2026, unlike the Army's Dark Eagle.

The "common" in C-HGB is the key — sharing the glide body between Army and Navy reduces per-unit cost and simplifies the supply chain for [[Mondaloy]] and other critical materials across both programs.
