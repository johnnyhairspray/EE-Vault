# Gait Analysis (Kinetic ID)

## 🔍 Summary
The automated process of identifying a person by the unique rhythm and mechanics of their walk. In 2026, AI-driven **markerless motion capture** allows the grid to extract a "Gait Signature" from standard CCTV feeds, mapping joint angles (hip, knee, ankle) and torso lean with up to 98% correlation to medical-grade sensors.

## 🧬 The "Kinetic" Piston

### 1. Silhouette & Skeletal Mapping
* **The Extraction:** Algorithms analyze a person's silhouette to segment individual body parts and build a 3D skeletal model in real-time. 
* **The Resistance:** Unlike facial recognition, gait analysis is effective even if the subject is wearing a mask, looking away, or walking in near-darkness.

### 2. Multi-Modal Fusion (2026)
* **The Mobile Link:** In 2026, kinetic ID is often "fused" with data from a subject's smartphone (accelerometers and gyroscopes) to verify that the person walking matches the "RampID" of the device they are carrying.
* **Distance Identification:** While HIIDE devices require proximity, Gait Analysis can identify a "Subject of Interest" from over 150 feet away using standard 4K security optics.

### 3. The 2-Second Walk (LSTM)
* **High-Speed Identification:** New regional-LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) models in 2026 can identify or re-identify an unknown person with as little as **two seconds** of walking footage.

## 🔗 The "Vetting" Loop
* **Sensor Capture:** A **[[Flock Safety Camera]]** captures a subject walking toward a secure facility.
* **Logic Collision:** The gait pattern is compared against a database of "Extracted" former employees or "Friction" activists stored in the **[[National Vetting Center]]**.
