# Wolf Pack

## 🔍 Summary
A massive, secretive biometric and behavioral database that stores comprehensive profiles on millions of Palestinians. It serves as the primary "Data Lake" for Israel’s AI-enabled military and internal security operations, populated through a network of peripheral surveillance tools.

## 🧬 The "Data" Piston
* **The "Wolf" Ecosystem:** Wolf Pack is fed by three primary sub-systems:
    * **Red Wolf:** Automated facial recognition cameras at checkpoints that enroll faces into the database.
    * **Blue Wolf:** A smartphone app used by soldiers to photograph and instantly identify Palestinians, often gamified with units receiving prizes for the most photos captured.
    * **White Wolf:** A version of the app provided to Israeli settlers in the West Bank to identify local residents.
* **Color-Coded Control:** The system provides soldiers with a color-coded signal (Green, Yellow, Red) indicating whether an individual should be allowed to pass, interrogated, or detained.
* **Shin Bet Integration:** Data is shared directly with **Shin Bet** to create "Intelligence Profiles" that inform the probability scores used by **[[Lavender]]**.
