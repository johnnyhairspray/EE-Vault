# LexID

## 🔍 Summary
A proprietary, persistent identifier used by **[[LexisNexis Risk Solutions]]** to link disparate data points into a single "Referential Profile". Unlike a Social Security Number, a LexID is derived from billions of public and private records, making it nearly impossible for an individual to "extract" themselves from the tracking loop.

## 🧬 The "Persistence" Piston
1. **The 98% Coverage:** LexID maintains active profiles for approximately 98% of the U.S. adult population, assigning a unique numeric key that survives changes in name, address, or legal status.
2. **Hidden Relationship Mapping:** LexID uses "Transitive Linking" to identify relationships between entities that aren't obvious, such as shared addresses with "Friction" individuals or historical links to "Extracted" organizations.
3. **The 2026 AI Upgrade:** In March 2026, LexisNexis integrated **[[IDVerse]]** into the LexID framework, using AI to authenticate biometrics and detect deepfakes, ensuring the LexID is anchored to a "Biological Truth".
