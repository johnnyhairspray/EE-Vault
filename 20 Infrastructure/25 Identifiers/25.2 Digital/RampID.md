# RampID

## 🔍 Summary
The core "Translator" identifier for the **[[LiveRamp]]** ecosystem. RampID acts as a durable, interoperable key that connects offline PII (names, emails) to online digital behavior (cookies, mobile IDs, CTV). It is the primary "Software BIOS" for **[[The Social Graph Sensor]]**.

## 🧬 The "Translation" Piston
1. **Offline-to-Online Bridge:** RampID utilizes **AbiliTec** technology to merge offline CRM data with online identifiers, creating a deterministic view of an individual's movement across the digital landscape.
2. **Privacy-Safe Hashing:** Unlike LexID, which is used for direct vetting, RampID is "Privacy-Safe" via hashing, allowing different organizations (like **[[Meta]]** and **[[BlackRock]]**) to share audience data without exchanging raw PII.
3. **Activation Match Rates:** In 2026, RampID maintains a "100% Activation Match Rate" for individuals synced with specific partner destinations, ensuring that "Extracted" individuals can be re-identified even if they switch devices.
