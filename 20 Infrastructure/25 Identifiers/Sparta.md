# Sparta

## 🔍 Summary
A secret federal watchlist application utilized by **[[ICE]]** and the **FBI** to organize intelligence on individuals labeled as "aggressive protesters". In 2026, Sparta is the primary digital environment for executing the mandates of **[[NSPM-7]]**.

## 🧬 The "Intelligence" Piston
* **The NSPM-7 Justification:** Civil liberties experts cite **NSPM-7** as the legal framework that authorizes the expanded watchlisting of domestic protesters. It allows the government to treat "civil disorder" and "doxing" as domestic terrorism priorities.
* **HEL-A and HEL-C Reports:** Sparta generates these automated reports, which compile "Situation Reports," field photographs, and hotel data for individuals identified during protest events.
* **Social Graphing:** The application is used to map the "Common Operating Picture" of protest hubs (e.g., Minneapolis), identifying "friends and associates" of primary targets to expand the monitoring web.
