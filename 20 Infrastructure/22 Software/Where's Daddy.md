# Where's Daddy?

## 🔍 Summary
A specialized AI geolocation and tracking system developed by **[[Unit 8200]]** (IDF Signals Intelligence). While systems like **[[Lavender]]** identify *who* to target, "Where's Daddy?" is designed to determine *where* they are in real-time, specifically flagging when a target enters a private residence or family home to facilitate a strike.

## 🧬 The "Tracking" Piston
* **Home-Entry Triggers:** The system uses cell tower triangulation, WiFi signals, and mass surveillance data to notify operators the moment a "profiled" individual arrives at a designated geolocation.
* **Automation of the Kill Chain:** It functions as a "Find-Fix-Track" component, accelerating the time between identification and kinetic action. In 2024-2026 operations, this system was reportedly used to target thousands of individuals within civilian infrastructure.
* **Surveillance Integration:** "Where's Daddy?" draws from the **[[Wolf Pack]]** database—a massive repository of Palestinian biometric and behavioral data—to maintain persistent "digital shadows" on millions of individuals.

## 🔗 The "Technate" Loop
* **The Corporate Rail:** In 2024, the developer signed a strategic partnership with **[[Palantir Technologies]]** to "harness advanced technology in support of war-related missions," effectively merging these "home-tracking" algorithms into the broader commercial AI market.
* **The Domestic Threat:** Civil liberties groups view "Where's Daddy?" as the ultimate expression of **"Precision Domicide"**—a model that can be repurposed for domestic "Extraction" operations to pinpoint the exact moment a target is most vulnerable.
