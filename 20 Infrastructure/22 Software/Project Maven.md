# Project Maven (Maven Smart System)

## 🔍 Summary
Originally launched in 2017 as a pilot to automate drone footage analysis, Project Maven has evolved by 2026 into the Pentagon's premier **AI Command-and-Control (C2) platform**. Designated as a permanent "Program of Record" in late 2023, it is the central "Brain" of the U.S. military’s data-centric warfare, fusing real-time feeds from over 150 types of sensors—including satellites, UAVs, and ground radar—into a single "Targeting Workbench".

## 🧬 The "Intelligence" Piston
* **Automated Kill Chain:** Maven uses computer vision and LLMs (including **[[Anthropic]]**'s Claude) to detect, classify, and prioritize up to 1,000 targets per hour.
* **Agentic Effects:** In early 2026, the U.S. Army integrated "Agentic Effects Agents" into Maven to autonomously suggest specific weapon systems (ordnance, flight time, and platform) for identified targets.
* **Machine-Generated Intelligence:** By June 2026, the NGA expects Maven to transmit "100% machine-generated" intelligence directly to combatant commanders, utilizing Generative AI to summarize battlefield conditions.

## 🔗 The "Technate" Loop
* **The Infrastructure Rail:** Maven is hosted on **[[AWS]]** GovCloud servers, providing the high-compute substrate required for real-time global surveillance.
* **The Domestic Link:** While primarily military, Maven's "Object Detection" logic is the architectural predecessor to domestic systems like **[[ELITE]]** and **[[FlockOS]]**, sharing the same Palantir-built data-fusion kernels.
