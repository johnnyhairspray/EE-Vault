# Palantir Gotham

## 🔍 Summary
The "Investigative BIOS" for tactical operations. Originally designed for counter-insurgency in Afghanistan/Iraq, Gotham was adapted for domestic use by the [[LAPD]] to integrate disparate criminal databases into a single, searchable visual map of "Criminal Networks".

---

### 🧬 The "Convergence" Logic
1. **The Tactical Piston:** Gotham acts as the "War Room" interface. It allows officers to search license plates, social media, and arrest records simultaneously to find "hidden connections" (e.g., who is talking to whom, where a car was spotted by an ALDR).
2. **The Extraction Cycle:** It turns "Street Data" (police interviews, field cards) into digital objects. In [[Operation LASER]], this was used to automate the creation of "Chronic Offender Bulletins" in minutes rather than hours.
3. **The Predictive Shift:** By 2026, Gotham has evolved to incorporate "AI-powered predictive policing," analyzing historical arrest patterns to "anticipate" where crimes—and specific "Chronic Offenders"—will appear next.

---

### 🔗 The "LASER" Connection
* **The Operation:** [[Operation LASER]] (Los Angeles Strategic Extraction and Restoration). A data-driven program (2011–2019) that used Gotham to identify and "extract" perceived criminal elements from specific neighborhoods.
* **The Fallout:** Despite being "shut down" in 2019 due to data inconsistencies and public outcry, the **extraction logic** survived. It was re-coded into the 2025-2026 [[NSPM-7]] framework for "National Vetting".
* **The Stakeholder:** [[JD Vance]] and [[Peter Thiel]]’s network views the "failure" of LASER not as a moral one, but as a lack of "Technical Efficiency" which the new Technate aims to fix.
