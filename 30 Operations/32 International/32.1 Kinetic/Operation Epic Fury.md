# Operation Epic Fury

## 🔍 Summary
A large-scale, coordinated military offensive launched by the United States and Israel on February 28, 2026, targeting the Iranian regime’s security apparatus. The mission, described by Secretary of War **Pete Hegseth** as "laser-focused," aims to dismantle Iran's ballistic missile production, its navy, and its nuclear infrastructure.

## 🧬 The "Kinetic" Piston
* **The Opening Salvo:** Kicked off at 1:15 a.m. EST with a synchronized wave of over 100 aircraft, including F-35s, B-2 bombers, and unmanned platforms.
* **High-Value Extraction:** The initial phase successfully targeted the heart of the regime, resulting in the death of Supreme Leader **Ali Khamenei** and Army Chief of Staff **Abdolrahim Mousavi**.
* **Strategic Demolition:** U.S. Central Command (CENTCOM) reported the destruction of over 330 ballistic missile launchers (roughly 70% of Iran's known arsenal) and the annihilation of its naval assets in the Persian Gulf.
* **Collateral Reality:** While precision-led, the strikes have caused significant infrastructure damage in Tehran and triggered retaliatory missile barrages across the region, resulting in thousands of casualties.
