# Operation Epic Fury

Large-scale coordinated military offensive launched by the United States and Israel on February 28, 2026, targeting Iran's military and nuclear infrastructure.

## The Strikes

Commenced 1:15 a.m. EST. Initial wave: 100+ aircraft including F-35s, B-2 bombers, and unmanned platforms.

**Outcomes (CENTCOM reported):**
- Death of Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei
- Death of Army Chief of Staff Abdolrahim Mousavi
- Destruction of 330+ ballistic missile launchers (~70% of Iran's known arsenal)
- Annihilation of Iranian naval assets in the Persian Gulf

**Civilian impact:** Significant infrastructure damage in Tehran; retaliatory missile barrages across the region; thousands of casualties documented.

Secretary of Defense Pete Hegseth described the mission as "laser-focused" on dismantling Iran's ballistic missile production, navy, and nuclear infrastructure.
