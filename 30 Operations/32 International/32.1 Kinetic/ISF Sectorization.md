# ISF Sectorization (Gaza)

## 🔍 Summary
The operational division of the Gaza Strip into five designated security sectors under the control of the **International Stabilization Force (ISF)**. 

## 🧬 The "Tactical" Piston
* **Sector Alpha (Rafah):** The first sector to undergo "Civilianization" in early 2026, with the ISF training 12,000 local police to replace IDF units.
* **The 53% Line:** Currently, the IDF maintains control over **53% of the Gaza Strip**, primarily in the north and the "Netzarim Corridor," pending weapons decommissioning milestones.
* **Military Basing:** Plans for a 5,000-person permanent ISF military base are currently under review by the BoP.
