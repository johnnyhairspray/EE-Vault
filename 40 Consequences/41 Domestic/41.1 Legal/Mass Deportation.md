# Mass Deportation

## 🔍 Summary
The operationalized state of the 2026 Technate, defined by the systematic removal of over **1,000,000 individuals annually**. This is the terminal "Endpoint" of the current administration's migration policy, where the fusion of AI-targeting (**[[ImmigrationOS]]**) and legislative funding (**[[One Big Beautiful Bill 1]]**) results in the physical extraction of populations from the domestic workforce and social fabric.

## 🧬 The "Consequence" Piston

### 1. The Numerical Milestone
* **1 Million Removals:** As of early 2026, the federal government has reached its stated goal of one million annual deportations, funded by the **$126 billion** enforcement allocation in the **[[One Big Beautiful Bill 1]]**.
* **Operational Template:** This scale is maintained through "High-Velocity Removal" protocols that bypass standard immigration court backlogs via expanded **Expedited Removal** authorities.

### 2. Operation Metro Surge (The Pilot)
* **The Minnesota Front:** In early 2026, **Operation Metro Surge** served as the proof-of-concept for urban mass-removal. Over **3,700 arrests** were made in the Twin Cities alone within three months, utilizing 3,000 federal agents.
* **Economic Friction:** The operation resulted in an estimated **$200 million loss** to the Minneapolis economy in January 2026 as schools transitioned to remote learning and local businesses were disrupted by "Surge" checkpoints.

### 3. Societal and Fatal Impact
* **The Fear Climate:** Beyond physical removals, the "Mass Deportation" endpoint has created a climate of total avoidance, with a sharp decline in border crossings and a "withdrawal from public life" by documented and undocumented immigrants alike.
* **Fatalities:** At least **40 deaths** have been attributed directly to ICE enforcement actions between 2025 and April 2026, including casualties during protest suppressions related to Metro Surge.
