# Renée Good

## 🔍 Summary
Renée Nicole Macklin Good (37), a U.S. citizen and writer, was fatally shot by ICE agent Jonathan Ross on January 7, 2026, during **[[Operation Metro Surge]]**. Good was acting as a legal observer, documenting ICE activity from her vehicle, when she was struck in the head by federal gunfire as she attempted to drive away from the scene.

## 🧬 The "Fatal" Piston
* **The Incident:** While documented by the agent’s own body-worn camera, reports indicate Ross fired three shots into Good's vehicle as it was turning away from him. 
* **The "Observer" Effect:** Her death marked a significant escalation in the conflict between federal agents and civil society, as Good was not the target of any enforcement activity but was actively monitoring the "Numerical Extraction" process.
* **Societal Result:** The killing of Renée Good served as the primary catalyst for the January 2026 Minneapolis protests, which eventually led to the death of **[[Alex Pretti]]**.
