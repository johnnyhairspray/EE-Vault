# Parady La

## 🔍 Summary
Parady La (46), a Cambodian refugee, died on January 9, 2026, just three days after entering ICE custody. His death was attributed to complications from fentanyl withdrawal and multi-organ failure.

## 🧬 The "Fatal" Piston
* **Systemic Failure:** Family and advocacy groups allege La begged for medical help for 24 hours while vomiting, but was denied water or appropriate withdrawal treatment. 
* **The Delay:** He was found unconscious in his cell on January 7; by the time he reached the hospital, he had suffered irreversible anoxic brain injury.
