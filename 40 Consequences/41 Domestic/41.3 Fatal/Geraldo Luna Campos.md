# Geraldo Luna Campos

## 🔍 Summary
Geraldo Luna Campos, a Cuban national with a history of bipolar disorder and anxiety, died on January 3, 2026. An autopsy ruled his death a **homicide** caused by "asphyxia due to neck and torso compression" following a struggle with guards while in solitary confinement.

## 🧬 The "Fatal" Piston
* **The Incident:** Witnesses reported Campos became unresponsive while being physically restrained by law enforcement. The autopsy revealed hemorrhages in his neck and abrasions on his chest and head.
* **Policy Friction:** Despite the autopsy findings, DHS initially claimed the death was a suicide attempt—a narrative later contradicted by the Medical Examiner's report.
