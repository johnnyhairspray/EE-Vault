# Heber Sánchez Domínguez

## 🔍 Summary
Heber Sánchez Domínguez (34), a Mexican national, died by suicide on January 14, 2026, after only six days in ICE custody. He was initially encountered following a simple arrest for driving without a license.

## 🧬 The "Fatal" Piston
* **The Trigger:** His rapid transition from a routine traffic stop to federal detention is cited as a primary factor in his psychological distress. He was found hanging in his sleeping quarters at approximately 2:05 a.m..
