# The 2026 Gaza Malnutrition Crisis

## 🔍 Summary
A catastrophic secondary consequence of the sustained 2023–2025 conflict. Despite the January 2025 ceasefire and subsequent peace deal, severe limitations on aid delivery have resulted in projected acute malnutrition for over **100,000 children** by April 2026.

## 🧬 The "Fatal" Piston
* **Casualty Milestone:** The cumulative direct death toll of the conflict exceeded **70,000** as of November 2025.
* **Screening Results (March 2026):** Recent UN screenings of 35,110 children found that 4% require acute malnutrition treatment, with 0.5% diagnosed with **Severe Acute Malnutrition (SAM)**.
* **Infrastructure Collapse:** Drinking water access in Gaza City fell below **6 liters per person per day** in early 2026 due to Mekorot pipeline damage and the destruction of the Safa Well Field.
