# The Trump-Mladenov Peace Plan

20-point framework for Gaza transition, endorsed by the UN Security Council as Resolution 2803 on November 17, 2025. As of April 2026, in Phase Two following verified return of all hostage remains in January 2026.

## The Framework

**Phase One (complete):** Ceasefire, return of all hostage remains.

**Phase Two (active):** Hamas weapons decommissioning — heavy weapons first — in exchange for staged IDF withdrawals from designated sectors. The ISF fills the security vacuum as IDF withdraws.

**Governance layer:** Establishment of the [[National Committee for the Administration of Gaza]] — a Palestinian technocratic body operating under Board of Peace oversight — with an eventual pathway to a "reformed" Palestinian Authority.

**Phase Three (planned):** Full Palestinian governance transition, permanent status negotiations.

## The Structural Analysis

The plan's framing presents a conventional post-conflict governance transition. The vault's analysis frames it differently: as the establishment of a sovcorp governance structure over a territory with no existing democratic accountability.

Key structural features:
- The [[Board of Peace]] is the supreme authority — chaired by Trump for life, unaccountable to any democratic body
- The Palestinian governing committee (NCAG) has no independent authority — operates within BoP mandate
- The ISF reports to the BoP, not the UN or any elected body
- "De-radicalization milestones" triggering IDF withdrawal are determined by the BoP — no defined criteria

The Jared Kushner connection: Kushner sits on the BoP Executive Board and has documented financial relationships with Gulf sovereign wealth funds (Saudi PIF $2B to Affinity Partners) whose capital is positioned to flow into Gaza reconstruction. The governance body and the financial beneficiary overlap significantly.
