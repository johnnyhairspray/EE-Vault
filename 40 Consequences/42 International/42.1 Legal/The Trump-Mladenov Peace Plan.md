# The Trump-Mladenov Peace Plan

## 🔍 Summary
A 20-point framework, endorsed via UNSC Resolution 2803, designed to end the Gaza conflict through a phased transition of power. As of April 2026, the plan has entered **Phase Two**, following the verified return of all hostage remains in January 2026.

## 🧬 The "Stabilization" Piston
* **Board of Peace (BoP):** A US-chaired oversight body led by Donald Trump and High Representative Nickolay Mladenov that manages Gaza's transition.
* **Phase Two Mandate:** Focuses on the decommissioning of Hamas weapons (heavy weapons first) in exchange for staged Israeli withdrawals.
* **The ISF:** Deployment of a 20,000-strong **International Stabilization Force** (ISF) to replace the IDF in designated sectors, starting with Rafah.
* **Governance:** Establishment of a "Technocratic Government" of Palestinian experts, intended to eventually cede control to a reformed Palestinian Authority (PA).
