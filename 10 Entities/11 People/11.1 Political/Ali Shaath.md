# Ali Shaath

Palestinian civil engineer and former Palestinian Authority deputy minister. Chairman of the [[National Committee for the Administration of Gaza]] — the technocratic body appointed by the [[Board of Peace]] in January 2026 to manage civil affairs of Gaza during the transitional period.

His professional background (civil engineering, PA administration) positions him as a politically unaffiliated technocrat — the deliberate framing of the NCAG as above Palestinian factional politics. Whether this is substantively true or cosmetic given the BoP's governance structure is an open question in the vault.
