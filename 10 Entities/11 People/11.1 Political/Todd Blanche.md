# Todd Blanche

## 🔍 Summary
The Acting Attorney General of the United States as of **April 2, 2026**, succeeding **[[Pam Bondi]]**. Formerly the Deputy Attorney General, Blanche is the primary architect of the "Blanche Memorandum," which reoriented the DOJ toward a "National Security First" model, specifically targeting cartels, immigration, and domestic "political violence".

## 🧬 The "Operational" Piston
* **The Judicial Override Acceleration:** Blanche took office specifically to accelerate the **[[14321 - Ending Crime and Disorder on America's Streets]]** mission, focusing on overturning judicial precedents that restrict mass civil commitments.
* **NSPM-7 Enforcement:** He issued the 2026 guidance to Joint Terrorism Task Forces (JTTFs) to prioritize "organized doxing, swatting, and civil disorder" as domestic terrorism, authorizing the use of the **Material Support** doctrine against protest leaders.
* **The "Individual Accountability" Doctrine:** Blanche pioneered a policy where corporate or non-profit entities (NPOs) are secondary to "individual accountability," a framework now used to personally target the donors and leadership of activist groups labeled as "Friction".
