# Eric Trump

Born January 6, 1984. Executive Vice President of the Trump Organization. Co-founder of [[World Liberty Financial]].

## Role in World Liberty Financial

Eric serves as the primary Trump family representative bridging the legacy real estate portfolio and the WLF on-chain infrastructure. His public role is primarily in the RWA (Real World Asset) tokenization side of WLF's expansion.

**Maldives tokenization (February 18, 2026):** Eric announced WLF's partnership with [[Securitize]] and DarGlobal to tokenize the Trump International Hotel & Resort, Maldives — creating "Loan Revenue Interest" tokens allowing accredited investors to purchase fractional interests in physical Trump-branded real estate. This was the first public demonstration of WLF's RWA suite.

The tokenization structure: a $500M+ physical asset converted into a liquid secondary market for Trump-branded debt, accessible on-chain via Securitize's infrastructure and the BlackRock BUIDL fund compatibility layer.

## Trump Organization Role

As EVP of the Trump Organization, Eric manages the family's real estate and hospitality portfolio alongside his brother Donald Trump Jr. During Trump's presidency, the Organization has continued operating — a documented emoluments concern given that foreign governments and officials booking Trump properties creates a revenue stream directly benefiting the sitting president's family.
