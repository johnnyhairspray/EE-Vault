# Pete Hegseth

Born June 6, 1980. Secretary of War (title changed September 5, 2025 — formally renamed the U.S. Department of War under Trump). Confirmed January 25, 2025 (51-50 Senate vote, VP Vance casting the tie-breaking vote — the first SecDef confirmed by a tie-breaking vote in history). Former Army National Guard officer; former Fox News host. Member of a church in the Douglas Wilson/Christian Reconstructionist network.

## The Pulp Fiction Prayer (April 16, 2026)

At a Pentagon worship service, Hegseth led the assembled personnel in a prayer he attributed to the "Sandy 1" Combat Search and Rescue (CSAR) team that rescued a downed U.S. aviator in Iran. He introduced it as "CSAR 25:17, which I think is meant to reflect Ezekiel 25:17."

What he then read was nearly word-for-word the monologue delivered by Samuel L. Jackson's hitman character Jules Winnfield in Quentin Tarantino's 1994 film *Pulp Fiction* — a fake Bible verse Tarantino himself had adapted from a 1973 Japanese martial arts film. The actual Ezekiel 25:17 is a single short sentence about divine vengeance against the Philistines. The Pulp Fiction version is approximately ten times longer and was invented for the movie.

Hegseth's version substituted "downed aviator" for "righteous man," "camaraderie and duty" for "charity and good will," and "my call sign is Sandy 1" for "my name is the Lord."

Pentagon spokesman Sean Parnell's response: *"Anyone saying the Secretary misquoted Ezekiel 25:17 is peddling fake news and ignorant of reality."*

The incident is analytically notable not because of the error itself — the CSAR teams apparently genuinely use this as a mission prayer, having absorbed it from popular culture — but because the Secretary of War recited it at an official Pentagon worship service without recognizing its origin, and the Pentagon then defended the misattribution rather than acknowledging it.

The prayer ends: *"And I will strike down upon thee with great vengeance and furious anger those who attempt to capture and destroy my brother, and you will know my call sign is Sandy 1 when I lay my vengeance upon thee."*

This is the theology of Operation Epic Fury dressed in Tarantino.
