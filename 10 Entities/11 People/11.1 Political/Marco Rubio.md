# Marco Rubio

Born May 28, 1971, Miami, Florida. U.S. Senator from Florida (2011–2025). Secretary of State under Trump's second term (confirmed January 2025). Simultaneous Executive Board member of the [[Board of Peace]] — the Gaza post-war governance body — making him both the nation's chief diplomat and a member of a private governance structure with interests in the territory he diplomatically administers.

## The Larry Ellison Connection

In 2015, Larry Ellison vetted Rubio for "fealty to Israel" with then-UN Ambassador Ron Prosor. Prosor certified Rubio's pro-Israel reliability. Ellison subsequently donated **$5 million to Conservative Solutions PAC**, Rubio's super PAC. This was the largest single donation to any Republican presidential candidate's super PAC that cycle.

As Secretary of State, Rubio oversees the same Middle East diplomacy — Gaza, Iran, UAE, Saudi Arabia — in which Ellison has documented financial interests (WBD media empire, Stargate, TikTok oversight, and the Oracle-Palantir Gaza governance architecture).

## Operation Epic Fury

Rubio served in his State Department role during the February 28, 2026 strikes on Iran ([[Operation Epic Fury]]). As Secretary of State he would have been party to or aware of the diplomatic pre-positioning for the strikes. His concurrent BoP Executive Board membership means the same person overseeing U.S. diplomatic posture toward the Middle East is also a governance authority over post-war Gaza reconstruction — territories directly affected by the strikes.

## Board of Peace

Per the January 2026 BoP structure, Rubio sits alongside Kushner and Ajay Banga (World Bank President) on the Executive Board under Trump's chairmanship. His State Department role provides the BoP with official U.S. government imprimatur while simultaneously allowing it to operate outside standard diplomatic accountability.
