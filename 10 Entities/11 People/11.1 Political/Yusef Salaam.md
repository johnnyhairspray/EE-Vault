# Yusef Salaam

Born 1974. Member of the New York City Council, District 9 (Harlem). One of the Central Park Five (now known as the Exonerated Five) — wrongfully convicted in 1989 of the rape and assault of a jogger in Central Park at age 15. Served seven years before being exonerated in 2002 when DNA evidence and a confession by the actual perpetrator cleared him and the other four.

Donald Trump took out full-page newspaper ads in 1989 calling for the death penalty for the five defendants — before trial, before conviction. After their exoneration, Trump continued to maintain they were guilty.

Salaam won election to the NYC Council in 2023 representing Harlem (District 9), re-elected November 2025.

In the 2026 legislative session, Salaam introduced 9 bills focused on police transparency and social media safety for youth.
