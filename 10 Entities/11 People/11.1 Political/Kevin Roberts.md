# Kevin Roberts

President of [[The Heritage Foundation]] since 2021. The architect of Heritage's transformation from traditional Reagan conservatism to explicitly post-liberal, Christian nationalist governance doctrine.

## The "Second American Revolution" Statement

Roberts delivered what became the defining statement of Project 2025's intentions at the Republican National Convention, July 2024:

**"We are in the process of the second American Revolution, which will remain bloodless if the left allows it to be."**

This statement is not a slip. It is the clearest public declaration by a Project 2025 principal that what is being pursued is a revolutionary restructuring of American governance — and that violence is a contingent outcome dependent on the opposition's response.

## The Post-Liberal Shift

Under Roberts, Heritage moved from a traditional conservative framing (limited government, free markets, individual rights) to an explicitly post-liberal one: that the current liberal order is irredeemably corrupt and must be replaced with Christian-ordered governance. His book *Dawn's Early Light* (2024) articulates this framework — arguing that "tolerating evil" under liberal neutrality is no longer acceptable and that the state must actively impose moral order.

This is [[Dominionism]] in policy form: the state as an instrument of God's law, not a neutral arbiter of competing interests.

## Project 2025 Leadership

Roberts oversaw the four-pillar Project 2025 structure:
1. Mandate for Leadership (900-page policy blueprint)
2. Personnel database (loyalty-vetted résumé pool)
3. Presidential Administration Academy (training loyalists to seize agency control)
4. 180-Day Playbook (agency-by-agency implementation, never publicly released)

When asked in 2023 whether Trump had read Project 2025, Roberts said: "He has."
