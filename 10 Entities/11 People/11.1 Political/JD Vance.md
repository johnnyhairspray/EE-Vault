# JD Vance

## 🔍 Summary
The **50th Vice President of the United States** (assumed office Jan 20, 2025). Vance is the first Millennial to hold the office and serves as the primary "Executive Piston" for the Silicon Valley-aligned wing of the administration. He is the direct bridge between the **[[Dark Enlightenment]]** and the West Wing, leveraging his venture capital background at **Mithril Capital** and **Narya Capital** to steer federal policy toward high-compute efficiency.

## 🧬 The "Convergence" Logic
1. **The Thiel-Trump Pipeline:** Vance’s rise was a multi-stage "extraction" managed by **[[Peter Thiel]]**. Thiel provided the initial 2011 Yale Law mentorship, hired him at Mithril in 2017, and personally brokered the 2021 Mar-a-Lago reconciliation with Trump.
2. **Administrative Architect:** In 2026, Vance is the lead advocate for the **[[Schedule Policy-Career]]** (Schedule P/C) transition, which reclassifies ~50,000 federal employees as "policy-influencing" to allow for swift removal.
3. **The National Vetting Piston:** Vance oversees the integration of **[[Palantir Technologies]]** into the **[[National Vetting Center]]**. This serves the mandates of **[[NSPM-7]]**, using AI-driven data analytics to "investigate and disrupt" domestic networks.
4. **The "Post-Immigration" Piston:** Aligned with **[[Alex Karp]]**, Vance argues that "Peak AI" makes mass immigration obsolete, justifying the use of automated vetting to facilitate total border closure and potential denaturalization of "fraudulent" findings.
