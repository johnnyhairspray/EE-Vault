# Rebekah Mercer

Born 1973. Director of the Mercer Family Foundation, which manages political spending for the family of hedge fund billionaire Robert Mercer. Among the most consequential dark money operators in the current conservative movement.

## Cambridge Analytica

Rebekah Mercer was the primary driver of the Mercer family's investment in Cambridge Analytica — the data analytics firm that harvested 87 million Facebook profiles without consent to build psychographic targeting for the 2016 Trump campaign and Brexit referendum.

The Mercer family funded Cambridge Analytica through its parent company Strategic Communication Laboratories, with Rebekah serving as a key board member and operational driver. Steve Bannon also held a board seat.

Cambridge Analytica is the documented proof-of-concept that consumer social graph data can be weaponized for political manipulation at scale. The Mercers funded the experiment.

## Media Infrastructure

**Breitbart:** The Mercer family was a major financial backer of Breitbart News — the publication through which Steve Bannon built the ideological infrastructure of Trump's 2016 coalition. Stephen Miller used Breitbart as his primary vehicle for the immigration restriction campaign that built Trump's base.

**Parler:** Mercer family funded Parler — the "free speech" Twitter alternative that became the primary organizing platform for the January 6th movement. Apple and Google removed Parler from their app stores after January 6; Amazon Web Services terminated its hosting. Parler was acquired by Kanye West in 2023 and subsequently shuttered.

The pattern across both investments: funding alternative media infrastructure positioned as immune to "cancellation" by mainstream platforms — building a parallel information ecosystem for the MAGA coalition.

## The Donor Network

The Mercer Family Foundation routes political spending through DonorsTrust and other dark money vehicles — the same laundering infrastructure documented across the vault's financial network. Rebekah is the operational manager of that spending; Robert Mercer provides the capital.
