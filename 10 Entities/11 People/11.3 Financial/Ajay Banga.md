# Ajay Banga

Born November 10, 1959, Pune, India. President of the World Bank Group since June 2023, nominated by Biden and confirmed by the World Bank's board. Former CEO of Mastercard (2010–2021). Indian-American; born in India.

## Board of Peace

Banga serves on the Executive Board of the [[Board of Peace]] — providing the financial institutional credibility layer to a governance body whose legitimacy is otherwise thin. The World Bank's involvement signals that international development financing could flow through or alongside the BoP framework.

The structural tension: as World Bank President, Banga is supposed to represent multilateral development interests accountable to member nations. As BoP Executive Board member, he sits alongside Kushner and Rubio in a private governance structure accountable to Trump. These roles are structurally incompatible if the BoP operates outside standard multilateral accountability frameworks — which it does by design.

Whether Banga participates in a purely advisory capacity or exercises governance authority over Gaza reconstruction financing is not publicly specified.
