# Jaymee Prichard

Born: 1992
Died: 10/25/2025

Civilian finance specialist at the Air Force Life Cycle Management Center, [[Wright-Patterson Air Force Base]]. Wife of [[Jacob Prichard]]. Killed by her husband on October 25, 2025 — her body placed in the trunk of their car and driven to West Milton Municipal Building where it was discovered by police.

Time of death not established. Three children survived her.

Her role in the AFLCMC — which manages acquisition, development, and sustainment of Air Force systems — placed her adjacent to the same defense procurement pipeline as her husband's Sensors Directorate work, though her direct programmatic connection to [[C-HGB]] is unestablished.
