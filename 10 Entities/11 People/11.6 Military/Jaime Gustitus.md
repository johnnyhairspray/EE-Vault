# Jaime Gustitus

Born: 2000 (approx)
Died: 10/25/2025

U.S. Air Force First Lieutenant assigned to the 711th Human Performance Wing at [[Wright-Patterson Air Force Base]]. Operations Research Analyst working in a top secret capacity with TS/SCI clearance. Specialized in mathematical and quantitative modeling for aerospace applications.

Graduate of the U.S. Air Force Academy (2022) with a Master of Science in Operations Research from the Air Force Institute of Technology. Also completed student-pilot training.

Her specific role — aerospace modeling with TS/SCI clearance inside AFRL — placed her in the targeting algorithm and trajectory calculation pipeline directly relevant to [[C-HGB]] guidance systems.

Killed by [[Jacob Prichard]] at approximately 2 a.m. on October 25, 2025, when he broke into her Sugarcreek Township apartment. No motive officially established. The Air Force Office of Special Investigations assisted with evidence analysis.
