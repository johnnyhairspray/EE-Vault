# William McCasland

Born: 1957 (approx)
Died: NA

Retired U.S. Air Force Major General. Commanded the AFRL Space Vehicles Directorate and Phillips Research Site at Kirtland Air Force Base from 2001 to 2004 — the directorate that funded [[Monica Reza]]'s [[Mondaloy]] research and the propulsion programs dependent on it, including the AR1 engine and [[C-HGB]] booster stack.

Named in the 2016 Podesta emails released by WikiLeaks as someone with "first-hand knowledge" of UAPs and crash retrieval programs. Associated with classified space weapons programs at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base. Considered a "gatekeeper" within the UAP research community.

Vanished February 27, 2026 — departing his Albuquerque home on foot, leaving behind his phone, prescription glasses, and wearable devices. A gray USAF sweatshirt was found 1.25 miles from his home but not confirmed as his. Extensive search involving FBI, Bernalillo County Sheriff, and military coordination produced zero trace.

Disappeared six days after Trump announced release of UAP/alien files, and one day before Operation Epic Fury began. His disappearance closes the chain of custody on [[Mondaloy]]: Reza held the patent, McCasland controlled the funding. Both gone within eight months of each other.

Albuquerque is home to Kirtland AFB and [[Sandia National Laboratories]] — the lead design agent for the [[C-HGB]].
