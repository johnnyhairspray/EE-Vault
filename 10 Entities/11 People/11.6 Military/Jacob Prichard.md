# Jacob Prichard

Born: 1991
Died: 10/25/2025

Civilian employee at [[Wright-Patterson Air Force Base]], serving as Acquisition Project Manager in AFRL's Sensors Directorate. Role specialized in technologies for air and space reconnaissance, surveillance, precision engagement, and electronic warfare — the same technological pipeline feeding targeting and tracking systems relevant to [[C-HGB]] guidance.

On October 25, 2025, killed [[Jaime Gustitus]] at her Sugarcreek Township apartment, then drove to West Milton Municipal Building where he opened his trunk to reveal his wife [[Jaymee Prichard]]'s body before shooting himself. Violence spanned three counties — Greene, Montgomery, and Miami.

No motive officially established. Air Force Office of Special Investigations assisted. Relationship between Prichard and Gustitus confirmed as professional acquaintances through shared work at Wright-Patterson. Full investigative reports not publicly released.
