### The Cursor Deal (April 2026)

SpaceX/xAI announced a deal with **Cursor** — the dominant AI coding tool — giving SpaceX the option to:
- Acquire Cursor for **$60 billion** later in 2026, or
- Pay **$10 billion** for collaborative work

Cursor's valuation trajectory: $2.5B (Jan 2025) → $9B (May 2025) → $29.3B (Nov 2025) → $50-60B (April 2026). ~20x in 18 months.

Announced less than a week before the Musk v. Altman trial. Two senior Cursor engineers had already joined xAI before the announcement.
