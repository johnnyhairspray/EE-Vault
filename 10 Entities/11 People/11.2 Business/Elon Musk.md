# Elon Musk

Born June 28, 1971, Pretoria, South Africa. World's wealthiest individual as of 2025-2026. CEO of Tesla and SpaceX; founder of xAI and Neuralink; co-founder of PayPal. Appointed Special Government Employee heading DOGE January 20, 2025; departed May 28-30, 2025 after 130-day SGE limit.

## The Haldeman Lineage

The ideological resonance between Musk's governance project and his grandfather's political history is documented, not speculative.

**[[Joshua Haldeman]]** — Musk's maternal grandfather — was the national director of Technocracy Inc. in Canada from 1936 to 1941. Technocracy Inc. proposed replacing elected governments with a "Technical Alliance" of engineers and scientists managing continental resources through energy accounting rather than the price system. The movement produced a detailed map of the "Technate of America" — a continental territory whose boundaries match territory-for-territory the lands Trump's expansionism claims: Canada as 51st state, Greenland, Panama Canal zone.

Musk has publicly quoted Technocracy Inc. language without attribution. His 2020 statement — *"Government is simply the largest corporation"* — is Yarvin's neocameralism and Technocracy Inc.'s governance-as-management framework, word for word.

**The Mars Technocracy quote (2019):** Musk tweeted *"accelerating Starship development to build the Martian Technocracy."* This is not metaphor or shorthand — it is the explicit use of the ideological term his grandfather organized his life around, applied to the colony Musk is building. The word "Technocracy" does not appear by accident in this context.

**The Mars governance post (December 29, 2024):** Musk posted on X: *"The Martians will decide how they are ruled. I recommend direct, rather than representative, democracy."* This sounds more benign than the 2019 tweet but operates identically in practice — whoever controls access to Mars controls who participates in that democracy. Direct democracy with a SpaceX-controlled immigration system is Technocracy Inc.'s model: the experts control the territory, the residents govern within the experts' constraints.

The loop closes: Haldeman's grandfather dreamed of a Technate of America governed by engineers. His grandson is building a Technate of Mars.

CBC documentary *The Making of Musk* (December 2025) documented the Haldeman-Technocracy connection directly. Musk has not addressed it.

## DOGE

Appointed de facto head of the Department of Government Efficiency as Special Government Employee — a designation limiting him to 130 days of service per year, chosen specifically to minimize disclosure requirements.

DOGE's operational blueprint is Curtis Yarvin's **RAGE** (Retire All Government Employees) framework — mass civil service termination, loyalty replacement, executive consolidation. Washington Post confirmed in May 2025 that Yarvin is the intellectual architect, with two DOGE advisers on record: *"Yarvin is the brain."*

**What DOGE accomplished in 130 days:**
- Eliminated 250,000+ federal jobs
- Seized access to Treasury payment systems, IRS databases, Social Security Administration records
- Attempted to dismantle USAID entirely (blocked by federal courts)
- Fired 177 NNSA employees including those at Sandia National Laboratories (nuclear weapon design agency); reversed, rehiring all but ~27
- Staffed agencies with young Musk/Thiel network loyalists as Special Government Employees
- Placed former Palantir employees in key positions across multiple agencies

Tesla Q1 2025 profits fell 71% during Musk's DOGE tenure. He departed May 28-30, 2025, citing disagreement with Trump over the One Big Beautiful Bill and its deficit expansion. Trump gave him a ceremonial key on departure.

Yarvin's public assessment of DOGE in March 2025: a C-minus. *"A chimpanzee orchestra trying to play Wagner."* His critique: insufficiently radical and operationally incoherent.

## SpaceX / xAI Merger (2026)

In February 2026, SpaceX and xAI merged into a single entity with a projected $1.25 trillion valuation, vertically integrating Starlink's global satellite internet infrastructure with xAI's Grok large language model. The combined entity controls:
- Global low-latency internet access (Starlink, ~6,000+ satellites)
- A frontier AI model (Grok) with direct access to X (Twitter) real-time data

The merger creates a private intelligence-communications-AI stack that rivals state capabilities in some domains. No regulatory review was conducted.
