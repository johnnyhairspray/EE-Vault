# Larry Ellison

Born August 17, 1944. Co-founder, Executive Chairman, and CTO of Oracle Corporation. Co-founder of Stargate LLC. Net worth approximately $250–350 billion, briefly the world's richest person on September 10, 2025 at $393 billion before Oracle's stock corrected. The most consequential tech billionaire most Americans cannot name — operating through a surveillance infrastructure empire, a media acquisition strategy, a political donor network, and a personal relationship with Benjamin Netanyahu that connects the vault's Israel, Gaza, and Board of Peace threads directly to its U.S. surveillance and AI infrastructure threads.
