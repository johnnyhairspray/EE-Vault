# Mark Zuckerberg

Born May 14, 1984. Founder and CEO of Meta Platforms. Co-founder of Facebook (2004). Controls Meta through a dual-class share structure giving him approximately 61% of voting power despite owning roughly 13% of total shares.

## The 2025 Pivot

After years of friction with the Trump political network — including the Cambridge Analytica scandal, content moderation controversies, and Trump's suspension from Facebook after January 6 — Zuckerberg executed a visible political realignment in late 2024 and 2025:

- Ended Meta's third-party fact-checking program in the U.S. (January 2025), replacing it with a "community notes" model similar to X
- Appointed Dana White (UFC chairman, close Trump ally) to Meta's board
- Met with Trump at Mar-a-Lago multiple times
- Meta donated $1 million to Trump's inaugural fund
- Meta reportedly funded renovation of the White House State Ballroom in late 2025

**PCAST appointment (March 2026):** Zuckerberg was appointed to the President's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology — formalizing Meta's relationship with federal AI policy and placing the CEO of a 3-billion-user social platform inside the White House's science advisory structure.

## Llama and Open-Source AI

Meta's Llama series — open-weights large language models released publicly — is Zuckerberg's primary competitive strategy against OpenAI and Anthropic. By releasing model weights openly, Meta commoditizes the frontier AI layer, making it harder for closed-source competitors to charge premium prices.

Llama 4 (released 2025) and subsequent versions are among the most capable openly available AI models. Their public availability means they are deployed across government, enterprise, and consumer applications without Meta having direct visibility into usage.

## The Cambridge Analytica Overlap

Peter Thiel was on Facebook's board during both the emotional contagion experiment (2014) and the Cambridge Analytica scandal (2016–2018). Thiel advised against fact-checking political ads throughout his board tenure. Zuckerberg's 2025 pivot toward less content moderation and closer alignment with the Trump political network represents a continuation rather than a reversal of that board-level advisory relationship.

## The Social Graph Asset

Meta's surveillance asset — the social graph mapping relationships, beliefs, behaviors, and emotional triggers for 3 billion+ users — remains the most comprehensive privately-held behavioral dataset in existence. The PCAST appointment raises the policy question of whether that dataset will be treated as a national security asset, and if so, under what oversight framework.
