# Balaji Srinivasan

## 🔍 Summary
Former CTO of Coinbase and a primary architect of the **"Parallel State"** theory. In 2026, Balaji is the leading voice for the transition from "Geography-based" nations to "Network-based" societies. He argues that the internet is a new "Primary Geography" where communities can align on values (code) rather than just soil (land).

## 🧬 The "Exit" Piston
1. **The Network School (2026):** Balaji has expanded his "Network School" to include physical campuses in **Miami** and **Singapore**, training a new class of "Techno-Administrators" to build startup societies that operate outside legacy state BIOS.
2. **The "Cloud First" Logic:** His 2022 book, *The Network State*, serves as the operational manual for 2026 "Exit" projects. It describes a five-step process for a digital community to crowdfund territory and achieve diplomatic recognition as a sovereign entity.
3. **Cryptographic Order:** Balaji posits that crypto is not just finance, but a "Code-Based Order." In the 2026 Technate, this provides the framework for extracting law and property rights from traditional courts and placing them on immutable ledgers.
