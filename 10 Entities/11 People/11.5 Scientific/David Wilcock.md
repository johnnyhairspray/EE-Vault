# David Wilcock

Born March 8, 1973. Died April 20, 2026. Age 53. American paranormal writer, YouTuber, media personality, and significant figure in the UFO disclosure movement. Books include *The Source Field Investigations* (2012) and *The Ascension Mysteries* — both New York Times bestsellers per publisher claims. Co-hosted *Cosmic Disclosure* with Corey Goode on Gaia (2015-2018). Regular presence on the History Channel's *Ancient Aliens*. Claimed telepathic contact with extraterrestrials; some followers believed he was the reincarnation of Edgar Cayce.

---

## Death — April 20, 2026

Boulder County Sheriff's Department responded to a 911 call on April 20, 2026 — a mental health crisis reported near the 1400 block Ridge Road, northeast of Nederland, Colorado. A man was found outside, armed, who had died from a self-inflicted gunshot wound. US Representative Anna Paulina Luna confirmed Wilcock's death on April 21 via X.

Official ruling: suicide following mental health crisis. No foul play investigation opened.

---

## The Final Livestream — April 18, 2026

Two days before his death, Wilcock hosted a YouTube livestream. His recorded statements:

*"People are disappearing. Scientists are going missing….It's a little bit scary."*

*"I'm excited to be here, you know, every day that I have on earth is a gift and a blessing, and I'm very grateful for that, because frankly, people are disappearing."*

*"You guys knew that I might still show up even though it was last minute and I've had a really rough week. Like, you know, that's kinda like every week, but somehow we keep on making it through all of this."*

He specifically referenced the deaths of other UAP/paranormal figures including Nick Pope and Erich von Däniken (died January 2026). He expressed that he was grateful to be alive given the pattern he was observing.

His final X post, April 19, 2026 — one day before his death:

*"My Dear Family, I am not yet sure if I am doing a show tomorrow. I've had some very intense stuff going on this weekend... Either way, I want you all to know how much I love and appreciate you!"*
