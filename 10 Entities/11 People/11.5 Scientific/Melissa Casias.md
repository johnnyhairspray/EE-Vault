# Melissa Casias

Born:
Died: NA

Administrative assistant at [[Los Alamos National Laboratory]] — birthplace of the atomic bomb and one of the most sensitive research facilities in the United States. Held security clearance commensurate with LANL access requirements.

Vanished June 26, 2025 — four days after [[Monica Reza]] disappeared. That morning she drove her husband Mark to work at LANL, told him she was heading to another location on site, then drove away. By 7:45 a.m. she was back at their home in Ranchos de Taos, told her daughter Sierra she had forgotten her security badge and would work from home. She was not seen again. No wallet, no phone, no keys.

Her disappearance clusters geographically with [[William McCasland]]'s — both in New Mexico, home to Kirtland AFB, Sandia National Laboratories, and LANL. The state is the second node of the defense aerospace research triangle.

Note: Her administrative role means direct programmatic connection to C-HGB or hypersonic programs is unestablished. Included for chronological and geographic pattern documentation.
