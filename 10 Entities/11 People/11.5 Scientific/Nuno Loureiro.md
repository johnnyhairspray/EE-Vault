# Nuno Loureiro

Born: 01/01/1978
Died: 12/16/2025

Portuguese-American plasma physicist and Director of MIT's Plasma Science and Fusion Center. Professor of Nuclear Science and Engineering. His team was reportedly approaching a functional breakthrough in nuclear fusion — the kind of development that would fundamentally restructure the global energy economy and render fossil fuels obsolete.

His connection to this cluster is energy-adjacent rather than directly propulsion or hypersonic: plasma physics underlies both fusion energy and the plasma sheath behavior of hypersonic re-entry vehicles. Whether that overlap is incidental or material to his death is unestablished.

Shot at his home in Brookline, Massachusetts on December 15, 2025, dying the following day. Killed by former classmate Claudio Neves Valente. No apparent motive established. No arrest information publicly detailed beyond identification of the suspect.

His death precedes the February 2026 cluster by approximately ten weeks — the earliest confirmed killing in the pattern.

Note: His connection to the hypersonic/C-HGB chain is the weakest of the cluster. Confidence in the pattern link is lower than for Reza, McCasland, Gustitus, and Grillmair. Noted here for completeness and chronological context.
