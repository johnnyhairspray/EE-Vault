# Anthony Chavez

Former engineer at [[Los Alamos National Laboratory]]. Missing since May 4, 2025 — the earliest disappearance in the New Mexico cluster documented in [[The Mondaloy Chain]].

Chavez disappeared from Los Alamos, New Mexico, leaving behind all personal items. No body found. No cause determined. The circumstances — leaving everything behind — are consistent with either a voluntary disappearance or an abduction.

He is the first documented disappearance in the cluster: Chavez (May 4) → [[Monica Reza]] (June 22) → [[Melissa Casias]] (June 26).

**Note on confidence:** Chavez's former-engineer status at LANL is documented. His direct programmatic connection to hypersonic or nuclear weapons programs is not established in available sources. He is included for geographic and temporal pattern documentation — not because a causal connection to the C-HGB chain is established.
