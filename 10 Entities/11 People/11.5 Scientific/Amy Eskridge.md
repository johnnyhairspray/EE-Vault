# Amy Eskridge

**⚠️ Pre-Mondaloy Chain case. Filed here for disclosure thread development.**

Born unknown. Died June 11, 2022, Huntsville, Alabama. Age 34. Officially ruled self-inflicted gunshot wound to the head. Neither police nor medical examiners have publicly released an investigative report.

Re-emerged as Case #11 in public discourse on April 17, 2026, as part of the cluster of deaths and disappearances of U.S. scientists connected to advanced research programs.

---

## Background

Eskridge held a double major in chemistry and biology from the University of Alabama in Huntsville, with additional expertise in electrical engineering, physics, and genetic engineering. She worked in leadership and technical roles across multiple research organizations.

Co-founded **The Institute for Exotic Science** with her father, Richard Eskridge — a retired NASA engineer specializing in plasma physics and fusion technology. Together they also operated **HoloChron Engineering**, under which they delivered presentations on gravity modification experiments, historical advanced propulsion research, and alleged black projects including the triangular antigravity craft known as the TR-3B.

Her research focus: antigravity technology — methods of manipulating or neutralizing gravitational effects, with claimed applications to space travel, energy production, and UAP propulsion.

---

## Stated Purpose of the Institute

Eskridge stated explicitly that she co-founded The Institute for Exotic Science to create a *"public-facing persona to disclose anti-gravity technology."* Her logic: *"If you stick your neck out in public, at least someone notices if your head gets chopped off. If you stick your neck out in private... they will bury you, they will burn down your house while you're sleeping in your bed and it won't even make the news."*

The Institute's website went offline after her death.

---

## Pre-Death Statements

In a 2020 podcast interview (published posthumously), Eskridge stated:

*"I'm scared, I'm tired. I need to disclose soon, man. I need to publish soon because it's like escalating. It's getting more and more aggressive. This has been going on for like four or five years, and over the past 12 months, it's been escalating, like more aggressive, more invasive."*

She reported being subjected to physical and psychological attacks, including what she described as a directed energy weapon attack that caused burns across her body.

In 2020, she stated she intended to present new foundational research on antigravity but required authorization from NASA before doing so.

---

## Intelligence Assessment

Retired British intelligence officer Franc Milburn investigated the threats Eskridge reported and her subsequent death. Milburn concluded her death was not suicide. His findings were submitted to Congress by independent investigators in 2023.

Journalist Michael Shellenberger testified before a public hearing on Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena that Eskridge was *"murdered by a 'private aerospace company' in the US because she was involved in the UAP conversation."*

Eskridge's father, Richard Eskridge, is publicly denying the death is suspicious.
