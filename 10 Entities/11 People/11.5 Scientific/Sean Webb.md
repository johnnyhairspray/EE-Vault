# Sean Webb

**⚠️ Status: Watch node. Institutional connections not fully verified. Possibly a bridge between documented vault networks and consciousness research/intelligence community ecosystem.**

Researcher, author, and systems engineer. Developed the Webb Equation of Emotion — a formal mathematical framework for modeling and manipulating human emotional states. Georgia Tech Advanced Technologies Development Center alumnus. Background in supercomputing and complex systems engineering.

## The Webb Equation of Emotion

**EP Δ P = ER** — Expectation/Preference compared against Perception produces an Emotional Reaction.

Underneath the equation sits the **{Self} Map**: every attachment an individual holds, ranked by power from 1-10. Emotional reactions can only occur for items on the self map. Core items (body, children, survival, identity) generate the most intense reactions.

The manipulation implication: if you can map someone's {self} map and control their perceptions, you can engineer specific emotional states with mathematical precision. The same framework that enables artificial empathy in AI enables targeted emotional manipulation of populations.

## Development Timeline

- **~2012-2013:** Developed framework, presented to MIT and GaTech via white papers requested by both institutions
- **2013:** Public-facing appearance on Smart Passive Income podcast — first documented public discussion of the equation
- **2017:** Published *Mind Hacking Happiness Volume I*; algorithms more formally documented
- **2023:** Published paper on ChatGPT psychological stability using the framework; set world-record Theory of Mind benchmark scores across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta models
- **2025:** Open-sourced the full algorithm set under AGPL-3.0

**Note on timing:** The 2013 development period is contemporaneous with the Facebook emotional contagion experiment (conducted January 2012, published 2014) and the data broker consolidation boom. Webb may have been working in parallel with, or in proximity to, the institutional actors building the manipulation infrastructure the vault documents.

## The Open-Sourcing Decision

Webb's stated rationale for open-sourcing in 2025: the LLMs already have sufficient training data to reconstruct this framework independently. Publishing it openly means the manipulation vectors become visible and can be monitored. Closed development would allow deployment without oversight or detection capability.

This is the nuclear deterrence logic applied to mind-hacking: mutually assured transparency rather than arms-race secrecy.

**Webb's explicit warning from the GitHub README:** The algorithms "can be used to automate the targeted emotional manipulation of individuals and groups of individuals, regardless of their interaction with any AI systems, so as to control foreign and domestic populations... This tech can be used to reduce suicides, or laser target the catalysis of them."
