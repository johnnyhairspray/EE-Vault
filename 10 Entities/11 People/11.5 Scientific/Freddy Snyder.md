# Freddy Snyder

Identified suspect in the killing of Caltech astrophysicist [[Carl Grillmair]] on February 16, 2026, at Grillmair's home in Llano, California.

Snyder had been reported trespassing on Grillmair's property with a loaded, unregistered rifle on December 20, 2025. Prosecutors dropped those charges by early February 2026. He returned and shot Grillmair on February 16 — eleven days before [[William McCasland]] disappeared and twelve days before [[Operation Epic Fury]].

The dropped charges followed by the killing constitute the most specific documented institutional failure in the cluster: a documented threat to a node in the defense research chain, charges dropped, threat executed.
