# Monica Reza

Born: Unknown
Died: NA

Aerospace engineer and materials scientist. Co-inventor of [[Mondaloy]], a nickel-based superalloy enabling oxygen-rich staged combustion in rocket engines, solving U.S. dependence on Russian propulsion technology. Spent decades as Technical Fellow at Aerojet Rocketdyne — the highest technical rank in the company — before moving to NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory.

Her work directly enabled the AR1 engine program and the [[C-HGB]] propulsion stack. Patent holder on Mondaloy. The material science underpinning America's only operational hypersonic weapon system exists because of her.

Vanished June 22, 2025 while hiking Mount Waterman Trail in Angeles National Forest, California. Last seen waving to a companion approximately 30 feet behind her. Extensive multi-agency search including helicopters, drones, and canine units recovered only a beanie and lip balm. No body found.

Case transferred to LA County Sheriff's Homicide Bureau Missing Persons Unit. Simultaneously, a Find a Grave memorial was created four days after her disappearance listing cause as "green burial" — while search helicopters were still active. The legal death declaration and active homicide investigation cannot both be accurate.

[[William McCasland]], who oversaw the AFRL program that funded her work, vanished eight months later — one day before Operation Epic Fury began.
