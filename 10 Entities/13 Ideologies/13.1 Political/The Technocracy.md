# The Technocracy

## 🔍 Summary
A system of governance where decision-making is shifted from elected officials to technical experts and algorithmic models. In the 2026 "Technate," this is achieved by "extracting" political friction (human bureaucrats) and replacing them with high-compute infrastructure managed by **[[JD Vance]]** and Silicon Valley stakeholders.

## 🧬 The "Extraction" Mechanism
1. **The Legal Piston (Schedule P/C):** The reclassification of 50,000 career roles as "Policy/Career" allows the administration to remove employees for "subversion of presidential directives," ensuring the bureaucracy functions as a seamless extension of the executive BIOS.
2. **The Compute Piston (Stargate):** The $500B **[[The Stargate Project]]** provides the physical hardware necessary to run the **[[Palantir Foundry]]** ontologies that track every "Human Object" within the domestic grid.
3. **The Sovereign Piston:** Utilizing **[[Red Caesarism]]** and **[[Neocameralism]]**, the Technocracy treats the United States as a "Sovcorp" (Sovereign Corporation) where **[[JD Vance]]** acts as the COO, overseeing the efficiency of the "National Asset".
