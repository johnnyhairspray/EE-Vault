# Antifa

## 🔍 Summary
A decentralized, autonomous movement rooted in anti-fascist and anarchist philosophies. In the 2026 state BIOS, "Antifa" has been codified as a **Domestic Terrorist Organization** via the September 22, 2025 Executive Order. This transition from a broad ideology into a specific legal target allows federal agencies to treat protest participation as "material support" for a terrorist enterprise.

## 🧬 The "Systemic" Piston
* **Legal Trigger:** The **September 2025 Executive Order** characterizes Antifa as a "militarist, anarchist enterprise". It directs federal agencies to "investigate, disrupt, and dismantle" all operations and funding sources.
* **Operational Framework:** **[[NSPM-7]]** provides the tactical roadmap, expanding the definition of domestic terrorism to include "organized campaigns of targeted intimidation," doxing, and civil disorder.
* **The "Indicia" Rule:** Under NSPM-7, the DOJ and FBI are authorized to flag individuals based on "ideological markers" like anti-capitalist rhetoric or specific protest attire, which are used to populate the **[[Sparta]]**, **[[Reaper]]**, and **[[Grapevine]]** watchlists.
