# Dark Enlightenment (Neoreaction / NRx)

## 🔍 Summary
An anti-democratic, pro-hierarchy movement that views the state not as a public service, but as a "Govcorp"—a joint-stock corporation managed by a CEO/Sovereign rather than a voting populace.

---

### 🧬 The "Convergence" Logic
1. **The RAGE Mechanism:** (Retire All Government Employees). Curtis Yarvin’s blueprint for dismantling the "Cathedral" (the administrative state).
2. **Patchwork:** The goal of replacing nation-states with a "spiderweb of sovereign mini-countries" run by corporations.
3. **The Exit:** The belief that "Voice" (voting) is a failure; "Exit" (building new systems like [[USD1]] or [[The_Technate]]) is the only solution.
