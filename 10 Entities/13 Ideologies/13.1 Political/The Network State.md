# The Network State

## 🔍 Summary
A concept popularized by Balaji Srinivasan (former CTO of Coinbase) that defines a new form of political organization: a "cloud-first, land-last" country. It begins as an online community aligned on a "moral purpose," proves its legitimacy through on-chain metrics, and eventually crowdfunds physical territory to seek diplomatic recognition.

## 🧬 The "Extraction" Logic
1. **The Cloud-First Piston:** Argues that the internet is the new "Primary Geography." By moving the BIOS of society (law, currency, identity) onto the ledger, the physical state becomes an "App" that can be swapped out if it becomes inefficient.
2. **The Moral Innovation Rail:** Unlike legacy nations based on ethnicity or borders, Network States are "One-Commandment" societies (e.g., "A state for keto," "A state for longevity"). This provides the ideological substrate for the Technate's **[[Transhumanism]]** goals.
3. **The Exit Strategy:** Instead of "Voice" (voting), the Network State uses "Exit" (migrating to a better system). This is the macro-scale application of **[[Peter Thiel]]**’s belief that democracy is a "zombie" system.
