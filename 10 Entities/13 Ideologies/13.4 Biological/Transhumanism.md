# Transhumanism ($H+$)

## 🔍 Summary
A technoscientific philosophy that advocates for the proactive use of technology—genetics, AI, and nanotechnology—to radically enhance human physical and cognitive abilities. In 2026, Transhumanism has shifted from a fringe subculture to a "Secular Faith" for the Technate, viewing the "Post-Human" state as the only way to survive the coming **[[Accelerationism]]** singularity.

---

### 🧬 The "Extraction" Mechanism
1. **The Biological Piston:** Treats death as a "technical glitch" rather than a natural end. In the 2026 grid, this justifies massive state/venture investment into "Longevity Rails" for the elite, while the "Human Object" is managed for baseline efficiency.
2. **The Neuro-Interface Rail:** Through firms like **[[Neuralink]]**, Transhumanism seeks to eliminate the "latency" of human speech. By merging the brain with the **[[xAI]]** substrate, it aims to create a "Cerebral HUD" for the Technate’s administrators.
3. **The Meritocratic Divergence:** Provides the philosophical "Hard Code" for the split between the "Enhanced" (who manage the grid) and the "Un-augmented" (who are vetted by it). This is the technical implementation of **[[Nick Land]]**'s "Hyper-racism".

---

### 🔗 The "Lineage" Piston
* **The Haldeman BIOS:** The ideology traces back to the "Technate" movement of **[[Joshua Haldeman]]**, who viewed the human body as a mechanical system to be optimized.
* **The Musk Mandate:** **[[Elon Musk]]** frames Transhumanism as a civilizational insurance policy. His 2026 merger of SpaceX and xAI is designed to provide the "Compute and Transport" required for a trans-planetary post-human species.
