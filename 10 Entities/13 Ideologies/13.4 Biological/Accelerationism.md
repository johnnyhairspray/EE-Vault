# Accelerationism (e/acc)

## 🔍 Summary
Specifically **Effective Accelerationism**. The belief that technological progress (especially AI) is an unstoppable evolutionary force that should be sped up at any cost. It views human "safety" concerns as "Decelerationist" (de-acc) friction.

---

### 🧬 The "Convergence" Logic
1. **The Gas Pedal:** Provides the moral justification for the $500B **[[The Stargate Project]]**. If AI is "inevitable," then any legal or environmental "bottleneck" (like EPA regulations) must be removed to win the race.
2. **The Post-Human Piston:** e/acc followers often view AI as the next step in "consciousness." This devalues the individual "Human Object" in the **[[Palantir Foundry]]** grid, seeing them only as data points for the larger intelligence engine.
3. **The Market-State:** Views the "Market" as a superior intelligence to "Government." In 2026, this drives the "Nationalization" of AGI, where the corporation and the state merge into a single "Intelligence Machine".
