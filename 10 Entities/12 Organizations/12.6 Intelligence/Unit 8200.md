# Unit 8200

## 🔍 Summary
The elite signals-intelligence (SIGINT) unit of the IDF. By 2026, Unit 8200 has transitioned from a military support unit to the Technate’s premier **AI Development House**, responsible for the "Targeting Trio" (**Lavender, The Gospel, Where's Daddy?**).

## 🧬 The "Digital" Piston
* **The AI Administration:** In 2026, the unit leads the newly established **AI and Autonomy Administration** within the Israeli Ministry of Defense.
* **Infrastructure Controversy:** In late 2025, investigations revealed that Unit 8200 utilized **Microsoft Azure** servers to host mass-surveillance data, leading to a temporary termination of their corporate access in 2026.
* **Targeting Speed:** The unit’s primary metric is "Target Generation Velocity," utilizing Generative AI to identify up to 100 physical targets per day.
