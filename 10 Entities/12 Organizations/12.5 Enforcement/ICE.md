# ICE (U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement)

## 🔍 Summary
The principal investigative and enforcement arm of the Department of Homeland Security (DHS). In 2026, ICE is executing a **$38.3 billion** infrastructure build-out known as the **"Detention Reengineering Initiative"**. This shift replaces inefficient local jail contracts with 34 massive, federal-owned "processing and detention hubs" capable of holding up to 100,000 individuals simultaneously to support a campaign of 1,000,000 removals per year.

## 🧬 The "Enforcement" Piston

### 1. The Warehouse Model (2026)
* **Processing Centers:** 16 regional facilities designed to hold 1,000–1,500 people for 3–7 days for initial biometric "extraction" and medical exams.
* **Detention Complexes:** 8 large-scale hubs holding up to 10,000 people each. These facilities include custom wastewater treatment, medical wings, and video-court rooms for rapid "remote" deportation hearings.

### 2. ImmigrationOS (The Digital BIOS)
* **Data Fusion:** A 2026-era AI platform that aggregates data from IRS records, Social Security filings, passport databases, and **[[FlockOS]]** license plate readers to flag non-citizens in real-time.
* **Lifecycle Management:** Automates the flagging of visa holders for "database inconsistencies" or "tax reporting errors," triggering immediate enforcement actions through high-fidelity "targets" identified by ad-tech and big data analytics.

### 3. HSI & Semantic Search
* **Homeland Security Investigations (HSI):** Utilizing LLM-powered "Semantic Search" and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to analyze Reports of Investigation (I-213s) and financial records to map transnational "friction" networks.
* **Ad-Tech Exploitation:** ICE utilizes commercial ad-tech location data to track and prioritize "targets with high fidelity," ensuring agents know exactly where a subject is located before an extraction begins.

## 🔗 The "Vetting" Loop
* **The Identifier Link:** ICE agents use **[[HIIDE Devices]]** in the field to collide biological data against the **[[LexID]]** and **[[National Vetting Center]]** watchlists.
* **The State Link:** Under the 2026 budget, ICE has fully integrated with the **HART (Homeland Advanced Recognition Technology)** system, ensuring seamless biometric verification across all enforcement missions.
