# International Stabilization Force (ISF)

## 🔍 Summary
A multi-national security force authorized by the **[[Board of Peace]]** in January 2026 to maintain order and oversee the disarmament of armed factions in Gaza. It is designed to act as the "Kinetic Buffer" that replaces IDF units "sector by sector" as de-radicalization milestones are met.

## 🧬 The "Enforcement" Piston
* **Composition:** Led by a U.S. General, with troop contributions from **Indonesia, Morocco, Kazakhstan, and Albania**.
* **Mandate:** Authorized to use "all necessary measures" to protect humanitarian corridors and the **[[NCAG]]** technocratic hubs.
* **Local Integration:** Currently training a **12,000-strong Palestinian Police Force** to take over neighborhood-level enforcement by late 2026.
