# Board of Peace (BoP)

## 🔍 Summary
The supreme oversight body for the postwar transition of the Gaza Strip, formally established in January 2026. Headquartered in a U.S.-led framework, the BoP holds absolute authority over the creation, modification, or dissolution of subsidiary entities in Gaza, independent of standard UN or PA constraints.

## 🧬 The "Governance" Piston
* **The Lifetime Chair:** **[[Donald Trump]]** serves as Chairman with no term limits and sole authority to nominate his successor.
* **High Representative for Gaza:** **[[Nickolay Mladenov]]** manages daily operations and diplomatic coordination with regional partners like Egypt and Israel.
* **Executive Board:** Includes high-level political and financial actors such as **[[Marco Rubio]]**, **[[Jared Kushner]]**, and World Bank President **[[Ajay Banga]]**, signaling a "Business-First" reconstruction model.
* **Mandate:** Oversees the disarmament of Hamas, the verification of "Terror-Free" zones, and the distribution of reconstruction billions.
