# National Committee for the Administration of Gaza (NCAG)

## 🔍 Summary
A 12-to-15-member Palestinian technocratic body appointed in January 2026 to manage the day-to-day civil affairs of the Gaza Strip during the transitional phase. It is intended to function as a non-partisan bridge between the current ceasefire and the eventual return of a "reformed" Palestinian Authority.

## 🧬 The "Administrative" Piston
* **Leadership:** Led by **[[Ali Shaath]]**, a civil engineer and former PA deputy minister.
* **Portfolio Structure:** The committee is divided into specialized roles to bypass political gridlock:
    * **Security/Interior:** Sami Nasman (a veteran PA officer and Hamas critic).
    * **Economy/Industry:** Aed Abu Ramadan.
    * **Health:** Aed Yaghi.
* **The Cairo Base:** As of April 2026, many committee members remain based in Cairo, coordinating aid entry while the **[[ISF]]** secures operational zones for their physical entry into the Strip.
