# NNSA (National Nuclear Security Administration)

Semi-autonomous agency within the Department of Energy. Established 2000. Responsible for the U.S. nuclear weapons stockpile, nuclear nonproliferation, and naval nuclear propulsion. Manages three national laboratories: [[Sandia National Laboratories]], [[Los Alamos National Laboratory]], and Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory.

## The DOGE Disruption (February 2026)

In February 2026, DOGE fired approximately **177 NNSA employees** — including personnel at [[Sandia National Laboratories]], the lead design agent for the [[C-HGB]] hypersonic glide body. The firings were almost entirely reversed, with all but approximately 27 employees rehired.

The Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists noted the firings gave DOGE access to Sandia's full personnel roster during the window of disruption — which occurred during the same month as the [[C-HGB]] program's transition to full operational deployment and [[Operation Epic Fury]].

Whether the DOGE/NNSA disruption was targeted, incidental, or unrelated to operational weapons deployment is documented as an open question in [[The Mondaloy Chain]].
