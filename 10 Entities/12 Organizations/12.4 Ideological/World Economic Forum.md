# World Economic Forum (WEF)

Annual forum founded 1971 by Klaus Schwab. Headquarters in Cologny, Geneva. The Davos conference — held annually in January in Davos, Switzerland — gathers approximately 2,500–3,000 political leaders, corporate executives, academics, and journalists. More transparent than [[Bilderberg Group]] but serving an overlapping coordination function.

## What the WEF Actually Is

The WEF is frequently mischaracterized in both directions — either as a benign international talking shop or as the operational headquarters of a global government conspiracy. Neither is accurate.

What it actually is: the most public-facing layer of the same elite coordination system documented across the vault. It publishes its agenda, its participants list, and its reports. It advocates explicitly for "stakeholder capitalism" — the position that corporations should optimize for a broader set of stakeholders than just shareholders, including governments, civil society, and workers.

The "Great Reset" — the WEF's post-COVID economic framework advocating a restructuring of capitalism toward stakeholder models — became a significant target of right-wing conspiracy culture in 2020–2021. The actual Great Reset documentation is publicly available and advocates for things like green energy transition, reduced inequality, and corporate accountability. The gap between what it actually proposes and how it was characterized on social media is significant.

## The Vault Relevance

The WEF is analytically relevant to this vault primarily as:

**The mirror structure:** WEF represents the "liberal internationalist" version of elite coordination — transparent, stakeholder-focused, multilateral — while the NRx/sovcorp network represents a competing vision of post-democratic governance that is explicitly anti-WEF. The ideological conflict between Davos globalism and tech-nationalist sovcorp thinking is real, but both arrive at reduced democratic accountability from different directions.

**Personnel overlap:** Many of the same actors documented in this vault attend both WEF and Bilderberg. Larry Fink (BlackRock), major tech CEOs, central bank governors, and Western political leaders cycle through both forums. The coordination function is distributed across multiple structures, not concentrated in one.

**The "clean room" parallel:** WEF's consensus-building function is the transparent version of what Bilderberg does privately. Policy positions that emerge from Davos as announced corporate commitments have often been pre-aligned through private forums.

## Distinction from NRx

The WEF and the NRx/sovcorp network documented in this vault are ideological opponents, not collaborators. Yarvin, Thiel, and Balaji explicitly frame globalist institutions like the WEF and UN as the enemy. The conflict is real — but both sides of it arrive at a world with less democratic accountability, just governed by different elites through different mechanisms.
