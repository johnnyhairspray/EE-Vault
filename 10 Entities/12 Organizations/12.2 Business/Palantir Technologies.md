# Palantir Technologies

## 🔍 Summary
The "Data BIOS" of the US government. Co-founded by **[[Peter Thiel]]**, Palantir provides the AI-driven software ecosystem (**Gotham**, **Foundry**, **AIP**) that integrates disparate data silos into a single, searchable interface for military, intelligence, and domestic enforcement. By 2026, it has become the definitive infrastructure for the "Technate-standard" data grid, facilitating automated target nomination and population management.

---

### 🧬 The "Convergence" Logic
1. **The ImmigrationOS Piston:** In 2025, Palantir secured a **$30,000,000** contract to build the "Immigration Lifecycle Operating System". This platform provides "near real-time visibility" into the entire migration pipeline—from initial sensor detection at the border to final physical deportation.
2. **The ELITE Tool:** (Enhanced Leads Identification & Targeting for Enforcement). This specialized mobile app allows field agents to "collide" federal data with non-traditional records (including **Medicaid and HHS** data) to map high-density neighborhoods for raids and assign "address confidence scores" to extraction targets.
3. **The $1 Billion Extraction Rail:** In February 2026, the DHS awarded Palantir a **$1 Billion Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA)**. This non-competed contract establishes Palantir as the default analytics layer for **CBP, ICE, FEMA, and CISA**, allowing these agencies to deploy AI tools without individual bidding processes.

---

### 🔗 Active 2026 Connections
* **Administrative (NSPM-7):** Acts as the technical foundry for the **National Vetting Center**, enabling the state to cross-reference private consumer data with federal watchlists to identify "ideological friction".
* **Technological (PFCS Forward):** In February 2026, Palantir received DISA authorization for **PFCS Forward**. This "hardware-agnostic" accreditation allows their surveillance stack to run on any device at the "Tactical Edge"—effectively turning **[[The IoT]]** and mobile vehicles into a persistent enforcement grid.
* **Political (The Karp Doctrine):** CEO **[[Alex Karp]]** has positioned the company as the "patriotic" alternative to Silicon Valley, arguing at Davos 2026 that AI will naturally empower "vocational" workers while disrupting "humanities-trained" populations.
* **Institutional Resistance:** On March 24, 2026, **NYC Health + Hospitals**—the largest municipal healthcare system in the U.S.—announced it would **not renew** its contract with Palantir (expiring October 2026) following public pressure over the company's role in "militarized violence" and mass deportations.
