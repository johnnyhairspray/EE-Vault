# Brain Co.

AI startup incubated by [[Affinity Partners]] (Jared Kushner's private equity firm) and investor Elad Gil. Founded approximately September 2025. San Francisco-based. Targets large businesses and governments for AI deployment.

**Co-founders:**
- **Luis Videgaray** — former Mexican Foreign Minister and Finance Minister. The person who organized Trump's surprise visit to Mexico City in August 2016 during the presidential campaign, which temporarily boosted Trump's poll numbers.
- **Eric Wu** — founder of Opendoor (PropTech)

## The Vault Significance

The structure is: Kushner co-founds a stablecoin infrastructure (WLF) that receives Gulf sovereign capital → simultaneously incubates an AI company that sells to governments → co-founded with a former foreign minister of a country whose relationship with the Trump administration Kushner informally advises on.

Luis Videgaray's involvement is specifically notable: he has deep existing relationships with Gulf states (Saudi Arabia, UAE) from his government work, and his co-founding of a Kushner-backed AI company targeting government clients creates a pipeline where Affinity's Gulf investor network could simultaneously be clients of Brain Co.'s government AI products.

A former foreign minister co-founding a government-facing AI company with the president's son-in-law, who is also an informal diplomatic envoy, is a FARA-adjacent structure without a clear legal mechanism to regulate it.
