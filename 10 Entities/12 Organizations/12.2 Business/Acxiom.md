# Acxiom

## 🔍 Summary
A legacy data marketing and services company owned by **IPG**. Following the 2018 spin-off of its data connectivity wing (now **[[LiveRamp]]**), Acxiom focused on the extraction and management of deep consumer behavioral data for the global marketing industry.

## 🧬 The "Behavioral" Piston
1. **The Behavioral Rail:** Manages massive consumer databases used to predict everything from political leanings to health risks.
2. **Legacy Infrastructure:** Founded in 1969, Acxiom is the "Genetic Source" of the modern data broker industry, pioneering the use of phonebooks and payroll processing to build the first national mailing lists.
