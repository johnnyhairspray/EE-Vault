# Amazon

## 🔍 Summary
The world’s dominant logistics and cloud provider. In 2026, Amazon shifted its strategy to focus on **Sovereign Cloud** and **Global Satellite Connectivity**. The company officially announced it will discontinue its **Amazon One** palm-scanning payment services at all retail businesses on **June 3, 2026**.

## 🧬 The "Operational" Piston
* **The $50 Billion Federal Rail:** Starting in 2026, **AWS** is investing $50 billion to add nearly **1.3 gigawatts** of AI and supercomputing capacity across **AWS Top Secret**, **AWS Secret**, and **AWS GovCloud** regions. This provides federal agencies with advanced AI chips like **AWS Trainium** to accelerate national security missions.
* **Amazon Leo (The Satellite Rail):** Rebranded from Project Kuiper. As of March 2026, Amazon is facing a critical FCC deadline to deploy 1,618 satellites by July 30, 2026, but is currently projected to fall short due to launch delays.
* **TeraWave:** A 6 Tbps space-based network for global connectivity announced by Blue Origin in January 2026, designed to integrate with Amazon's LEO constellation.
* **The Biometric Retraction:** Following the June 3, 2026 shutdown of Amazon One, all biometric user data, including palm prints, will be automatically deleted.
