# G42

Abu Dhabi AI and cloud computing conglomerate. Founded 2018. Chaired by [[Tahnoun bin Zayed Al Nahyan]] — the UAE's National Security Advisor. CEO: Peng Xiao.

G42's portfolio spans healthcare AI, smart city infrastructure, cloud services, and government AI systems. It has become the primary vehicle for deploying advanced AI across the UAE and exporting Abu Dhabi's AI infrastructure ambitions to other countries.

## The Huawei / China Problem

G42 had documented ties to Huawei and other Chinese technology companies, raising concerns within U.S. intelligence that advanced American AI technology passed through G42 could be accessible to Chinese state actors. Under Biden, the administration blocked sales of advanced Nvidia AI chips to G42 on this basis.

**Microsoft $1.5B investment (2024):** Despite the Biden-era concerns, Microsoft invested $1.5 billion in G42 in April 2024 — reportedly after G42 agreed to divest its Chinese technology partnerships. That divestiture's completeness and verification has not been independently confirmed in public reporting.

## The Trump Chip Deal

Following Tahnoun's $500M investment in [[World Liberty Financial]] four days before Trump's inauguration, the Trump administration reversed the Biden-era chip restrictions. In November 2025, the administration approved an equivalent of 35,000 Nvidia GB300 processors for G42, with a pledge of **500,000 advanced AI chips annually** — enough to build one of the world's largest AI data center networks.

Multiple NSC officials raised national security concerns about G42's remaining Chinese ties. Trump fired six NSC officials in April 2025. David Sacks completed the deal over remaining objections.

## The WLF Board Placement

G42 CEO Peng Xiao was placed on the World Liberty Financial board without public disclosure, alongside G42 general counsel Martin Edelman. This was documented as part of the Tahnoun/WLF transaction — giving G42's leadership governance positions inside the Trump family's crypto infrastructure.

## The Stargate / UAE Connection

G42 is a partner in the **UAE Stargate** initiative (announced May 2025, opening 2026) — the international extension of [[The Stargate Project]] into the Middle East, alongside Nvidia and Cisco. This places G42 at the center of the same AI infrastructure build that Oracle and OpenAI are constructing in the United States.
