**See [[Jared Kushner]] for full analysis** — including the Saudi PIF overrule of its own screening committee, the $157.5M in fees collected against zero returns to investors, the Electronic Arts $52.5B LBO, Brain Co., the Albania and Serbia real estate entanglements, and the Senate Finance Committee FARA referral.

## Summary
