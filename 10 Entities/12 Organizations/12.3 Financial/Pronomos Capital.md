# Pronomos Capital

## 🔍 Summary
A venture capital firm founded by Patri Friedman (grandson of Milton Friedman) dedicated to bankrolling the creation of **Charter Cities** and autonomous jurisdictions. It serves as the primary "Liquidity Rail" for the **[[The Network State]]** movement, providing the seed capital for private entities to negotiate sovereign land deals in developing nations.

## 🧬 The "Sovereignty" Piston
1. **The Exit Incubator:** Pronomos does not just invest in tech; it invests in **Law**. It funds organizations that secure government approvals to build semi-autonomous zones where investors can draft their own commercial and tax regulations.
2. **The Global Reach (2026):** As of early 2026, Pronomos-backed projects are active or in negotiation in **Honduras (Próspera)**, **Palau**, **Ghana**, and **Greenland**—the latter being a "Libertarian Crypto State" initiative targeting rare earth mineral extraction for the AI boom.
3. **The Common Law Rail:** Its projects typically utilize **British Common Law** enforced by retired UK or US judges, effectively "extracting" the legal system of the host nation and replacing it with a private, for-profit judicial BIOS.
