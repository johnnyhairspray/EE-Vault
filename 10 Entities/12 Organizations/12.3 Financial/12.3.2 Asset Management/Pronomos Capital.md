# Pronomos Capital

Venture capital firm founded by Patri Friedman (Milton Friedman's grandson) in 2019. Backed by Peter Thiel and Marc Andreessen. Specializes in funding charter cities and autonomous jurisdictions — private territories negotiating their own governance, tax, and legal frameworks outside host nation control.

## What It Funds

Pronomos doesn't just invest in technology companies — it invests in governance frameworks. Portfolio projects negotiate with host nations to create semi-autonomous zones where investors draft commercial and tax regulations, often using British common law enforced by retired UK or U.S. judges rather than the host country's legal system.

**[[Próspera]] (Honduras):** The most advanced Pronomos-backed project. A ZEDE (Zone for Employment and Economic Development) on the island of Roatán, now in legal dispute after Honduras's 2022 repeal of the ZEDE framework — with Próspera filing a $10.775B ICSID arbitration claim against Honduras.

**[[Praxis]]:** The Network State project targeting Greenland as territory for a private oligarch-governed city-state. Raised $525M; 151,000+ "citizens" from 80 countries. Pronomos is the primary capital vehicle.

**Other active negotiations (2026):** Palau, Ghana, and a Greenland "Libertarian Crypto State" initiative targeting rare earth mineral extraction for AI infrastructure.

## The Ideological Significance

Pronomos is the financial infrastructure for Balaji Srinivasan's Network State theory and Yarvin's neocameralist framework made physical — private capital negotiating sovereign territory, replacing democratic host country governance with contract-based private administration.

The Milton Friedman lineage is explicit: Patri Friedman's grandfather's Mont Pelerin Society built the intellectual infrastructure for neoliberal governance via think tanks over 75 years; Praxis/Pronomos is attempting to build the physical infrastructure for post-democratic governance in a decade.

## The Greenland Connection

Trump's push for Greenland — with Ken Howery (Thiel's PayPal co-founder) appointed Ambassador to Denmark with the stated purpose of leading Greenland negotiations — directly benefits Pronomos's Greenland investment thesis. When Howery's nomination was announced, Praxis's official account posted: "According to plan."
