# World Liberty Financial (WLF)

## 🔍 Summary
The primary DeFi interface for the Trump family and the financial "I/O Port" for the 2026 Technate. WLF facilitates the tokenization of Real World Assets (RWA) and the issuance of **USD1**, a stablecoin designed to bypass legacy banking friction. In early 2026, WLF applied for a **U.S. National Banking License** to officially bridge its on-chain liquidity with the federal BIOS.

## 🧬 The "Convergence" Logic

### 1. The RWA Piston (Maldives Initiative)
* **Tokenized Yield:** On February 18, 2026, WLF partnered with **Securitize** and **DarGlobal** to tokenize loan revenue interests in the **Trump International Hotel & Resort, Maldives**.
* **The Extraction:** This allows accredited investors to trade "Revenue Interest" tokens in a $500M+ physical asset, effectively turning high-end real estate into a liquid, on-chain utility.

### 2. The Institutional Rail (BlackRock/Securitize)
* **The BUIDL Bridge:** Through Securitize, WLF assets are compatible with **BlackRock’s BUIDL fund**. This creates a "Liquidity Rail" where MAGA-aligned capital can be redeemed for institutional-grade US Treasuries with zero settlement latency.
* **Regulatory Shield:** The tokens are issued under **Rule 506(c)** (Regulation D), ensuring the project remains "compliant-yet-sovereign" while targeting high-net-worth "Stakeholders".

### 3. The Governance Gate (The Node Tier)
* **Staking Mechanics:** Per the March 2026 proposal, holders must stake **WLFI** for a minimum of 180 days to participate in the grid's governance.
* **Node vs. Super Node:**
    * **Nodes (10M+ WLFI / ~$1M):** Gain access to **OTC Conversion Pathways**, allowing direct 1:1 interaction with licensed market makers for USD1.
    * **Super Nodes (50M+ WLFI):** Unlock "Priority Partnership" status, effectively becoming the shadow-board of the parallel financial system.
