# Saudi Public Investment Fund (PIF)

Saudi Arabia's sovereign wealth fund. Founded 1971, dramatically expanded under Mohammed bin Salman's Vision 2030 initiative. AUM $925B+ as of 2026. Governor: Yasir Al-Rumayyan. Chaired by MBS himself.

## Relevance to the Vault

**[[Affinity Partners]] ($2B investment):** The PIF invested $2 billion in Jared Kushner's private equity firm in 2021, against the recommendation of the PIF's own screening committee. MBS personally overruled the committee. The fund returned zero profits through 2025 while generating $87M+ in management fees for Kushner. Senator Wyden concluded this functions as a payment to the Trump family rather than a commercial investment.

**Electronic Arts LBO:** PIF is the majority equity holder in the $52.5B EA acquisition brokered by Affinity Partners in September 2025. The transaction placed Saudi Arabia in majority control of a gaming platform with 150+ million monthly active users.

**SoftBank / Stargate:** PIF is a major LP in SoftBank's Vision Fund, which is one of the four founding partners of [[The Stargate Project]]. Saudi sovereign capital flows into U.S. AI infrastructure through this chain.

**Sports acquisitions:** PIF owns Newcastle United FC, Golf Saudi (LIV Golf), and numerous other sports properties — part of MBS's sportswashing strategy to normalize Saudi Arabia's international reputation.
