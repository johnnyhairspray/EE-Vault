# Founders Fund

## 🔍 Summary
A contrarian venture capital firm co-founded by **[[Peter Thiel]]** that prioritizes "Hard Tech" breakthroughs over incremental software. In 2026, it is the dominant financial force in the "New Defense" domain, managing over **$17 billion** in assets and functioning as the primary venture-incubator for the Technate's infrastructure.

## 🧬 The "Infrastructure" Piston
1. **The Defense-Tech Rail:** Through partners like **Trae Stephens**, Founders Fund has birthed the "Defense Unicorns" (e.g., **[[Anduril Industries]]**) that now provide the autonomous border and warfighting hardware for the administration.
2. **The Space-Compute Merger:** The firm is a lead investor in **Impulse Space** and **Varda Space Industries**, aiming to move high-value manufacturing and compute off-planet to avoid terrestrial regulatory friction.
3. **Growth III Fund (2026):** Utilizing its $4.6 billion late-stage fund (closed April 2025), the firm is currently aggressively funding the **[[The Stargate Project]]** ecosystem, ensuring the Technate has a proprietary hardware stack.
