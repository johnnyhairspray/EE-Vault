# Securitize

Digital asset securities company providing regulatory-compliant infrastructure for tokenizing real-world assets. Founded 2017. Headquarters in Miami.

## What Securitize Does

Securitize provides the legal and technical infrastructure to convert physical assets — real estate, private equity, debt instruments, commodities — into digital tokens that can be traded on blockchain infrastructure while remaining compliant with SEC regulations (primarily Regulation D and Regulation S exemptions).

The company acts as:
- **Transfer agent:** Managing ownership records for tokenized securities
- **Compliance layer:** KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) verification for token buyers
- **Issuance platform:** Technical infrastructure for creating and distributing tokenized securities

## The BlackRock BUIDL Connection

Securitize is the transfer agent for BlackRock's BUIDL fund — the world's largest tokenized money market fund (launched March 2024, primarily invested in U.S. Treasuries). This relationship established Securitize as the institutional-grade bridge between blockchain infrastructure and traditional financial assets.

The significance: when [[World Liberty Financial]] claims its RWA tokens are compatible with BlackRock BUIDL, it means WLF's tokenized products use the same Securitize infrastructure that processes institutional BlackRock assets — giving WLF's crypto products a veneer of institutional credibility through the shared technical layer.

## The World Liberty Financial / Maldives Partnership

On February 18, 2026, WLF, Securitize, and DarGlobal announced the tokenization of the Trump International Hotel & Resort, Maldives — the first major Trump-branded real estate asset converted to on-chain tokens. Securitize provides the compliance and transfer agent infrastructure; DarGlobal provides the property; WLF provides the distribution platform.

This deal is the proof of concept for WLF's stated RWA suite — demonstrating that Trump-branded physical assets can be converted into regulated digital securities accessible to accredited investors globally.
