# Oracle

Enterprise software and cloud infrastructure company. Founded 1977 by Larry Ellison. Headquarters in Austin, Texas. Market cap approximately $400B+ (2025–2026). One of the three founding commercial partners of [[The Stargate Project]] alongside OpenAI and SoftBank.

## Government and Intelligence Infrastructure

Oracle's national security footprint is extensive and documented:

**CIA C2E contract:** Oracle is a partner in the CIA's Commercial Cloud Enterprise (C2E) alongside AWS and Microsoft — providing cloud infrastructure for classified intelligence community workloads.

**Oracle National Security Regions:** Air-gapped cloud environments accredited for classified government workloads, including Top Secret/SCI. Marketed as "sovereign" infrastructure where sensitive data never leaves a controlled perimeter.

**March 2026 BOA with CIA:** Basic Ordering Agreement enabling seamless data sharing between unclassified commercial data sources and Top Secret intelligence domains. This is the documented mechanism by which commercially collected data enters the intelligence community's analytical environment.

**AI Data Platform for Federal Government (March 2026):** Oracle unveiled an AI platform allowing federal agencies to deploy frontier AI models including xAI's Grok within air-gapped national security environments.

## Palantir Integration

In early 2026, Palantir and Oracle finalized a deep integration moving Palantir's federal Foundry workloads entirely onto Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI). This combines Palantir's AI-powered targeting and surveillance software with Oracle's classified cloud hardware — the two dominant government data infrastructure companies operating as a single stack for federal intelligence and law enforcement workloads.

## Project Texas / TikTok

Oracle serves as the "Technical Trustee" for Project Texas — the arrangement under which TikTok's U.S. user data is stored on Oracle-managed infrastructure as a condition of the platform's continued U.S. operation. Oracle holds the data; Oracle has access to ByteDance's U.S. user data under this arrangement.

## Stargate

Oracle is one of four founding partners of [[The Stargate Project]] ($500B AI infrastructure initiative). Oracle's Redwood infrastructure provides the physical data center substrate. Larry Ellison personally appeared alongside Trump, Sam Altman, and Masayoshi Son at the January 21, 2026 White House announcement.
