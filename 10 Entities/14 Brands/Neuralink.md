# Neuralink

## 🔍 Summary
A neurotechnology company developing ultra-high bandwidth brain-computer interfaces (BCIs). Its flagship device, the **"Link"** (N1), is a coin-sized implant designed to be inserted by a custom-built **Tesla AI surgical robot**. In 2026, Neuralink is scaling toward commercialization, aiming to perform over 20,000 surgeries annually by 2031.

---

### 🧬 The "Extraction" Mechanism
1. **The Telepathy Piston:** The "Telepathy" interface allows users to control computers, smartphones, and robotic limbs through thought alone. For the Technate, this represents the extraction of physical labor—allowing "Neuralnauts" to interface with the grid at the speed of silicon.
2. **The Speech Restoration Rail (VOICE):** In March 2026, Musk confirmed that Neuralink is now capable of restoring speech by translating brain activity into audible words, specifically for patients with ALS and stroke. 
3. **The Blindsight Piston:** Currently entering human trials in 2026, the "Blindsight" project stimulates the visual cortex directly, bypassing the eyes to restore sight to the blind. This confirms the **[[Transhumanism]]** goal of viewing human senses as "upgradable hardware".
